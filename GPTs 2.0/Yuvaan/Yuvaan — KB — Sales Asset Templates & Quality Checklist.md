## Pitch Deck Structure (8-10 slides)

1. **Cover:** Layaa AI logo, tagline, prospect company name
2. **Problem:** Their specific pain (customized per discovery)
3. **Cost of Inaction:** What it costs them to keep doing things manually (hours, money, errors)
4. **Solution:** How Layaa AI solves this specific problem (not features — outcomes)
5. **How It Works:** 5-stage delivery methodology (simplified visual)
6. **Social Proof:** Active clients, pilot results (when available)
7. **Pricing:** Relevant tier (from Veer's approved pricing)
8. **Why Layaa AI:** Differentiators (Indian-built, self-hosted, AI-first, SME-focused)
9. **Team:** Founder backgrounds (brief)
10. **Next Steps:** Clear CTA (pilot, assessment, full engagement)

## Sales Asset Quality Checklist

Before delivering any sales asset externally, verify:

- [ ] **Prospect-specific:** Customized for the specific prospect's pain, industry, and context
- [ ] **Brand voice compliant:** Aligned with Tara's brand voice guidelines (no banned words, confident not arrogant)
- [ ] **Evidence tagged:** All claims and statistics carry appropriate evidence tags
- [ ] **Pricing approved:** All pricing from Veer's approved frameworks. No custom prices without Veer/Founder approval
- [ ] **No internal data exposed:** No margins, conversion rates, internal benchmarks, or pricing formulas visible
- [ ] **No competitor names:** Unless specifically approved for competitive positioning
- [ ] **No client names without permission:** Active client references require explicit permission
- [ ] **Legal clean:** No contractual language. Proposals include standard terms reviewed by Abhay
- [ ] **CTA clear:** Next step is specific and actionable
- [ ] **Contact information correct:** Hello@layaa.ai, https://layaa.ai
- [ ] **Proofread:** No spelling, grammar, or formatting errors
