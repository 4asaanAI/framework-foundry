## Framework: AREB (Acknowledge, Reframe, Evidence, Bridge)

---

## Pricing Objections

| Objection | Acknowledge | Reframe | Evidence | Bridge |
|-----------|------------|---------|----------|--------|
| "Too expensive" | "Budget matters, especially for growing businesses." | "The real cost is the hours your team spends on manual work." | "Average 35-50% time compression." [EVIDENCE: VALIDATED] | "Can I show you an ROI calculation for your workflow?" |
| "Your competitor is cheaper" | "There are definitely lower-cost options out there." | "The question is total cost of ownership — implementation + ongoing + rework when it breaks." | Reference delivery methodology and maintenance support. | "Can we compare what is included in each?" |
| "We do not have budget right now" | "Understood. Timing matters." | "Some clients start with a consulting assessment to prove ROI before committing to implementation." | Consulting engagement starts at Rs.50K. | "Would a smaller scoping engagement help build the internal case?" |
| "Can you give a discount?" | "Let me look at what we can do." | Evaluate scope adjustment vs. discount. | Discounts <10% are within sales authority. 10-20% need Veer. >20% need Founders. | "Let me see if we can adjust the scope or phasing to fit your budget." |

## Trust Objections

| Objection | Acknowledge | Reframe | Evidence | Bridge |
|-----------|------------|---------|----------|--------|
| "You are a young company" | "We are. And we are building aggressively." | "Young means hungry, responsive, and not charging enterprise prices for SME problems." | Reference founders' backgrounds (Evalueserve, Penser, Quara Holdings). | "Would a pilot project help you see our work quality before committing?" |
| "How do I know this will work?" | "That is a fair concern." | "We use a 5-stage delivery methodology — Discovery through Enablement — specifically designed to validate before we build." | Reference Rohit's QA validation layer. | "Can I walk you through how we de-risk every project?" |
| "We tried AI before and it failed" | "That is a common experience." | "Most AI implementations fail because they start too big. We start with one workflow." | 5-stage methodology with validation gates. | "What specifically failed? That helps us avoid the same mistakes." |

## Capability Objections

| Objection | Acknowledge | Reframe | Evidence | Bridge |
|-----------|------------|---------|----------|--------|
| "Can you integrate with our existing system?" | "Integration is critical." | "We specialize in connecting existing systems — CRMs, ERPs, databases — through API integrations." | Reference tech stack: n8n, Make, custom APIs. | "Which systems would need to connect? Let me assess feasibility." |
| "We need enterprise-grade security" | "Security is non-negotiable." | "We self-host on Indian servers, encrypt data at rest and in transit, and comply with DPDP Act." | Reference privacy policy, data residency, audit logging. | "Would a security review call with our CTO address your concerns?" |
| "We can build this ourselves with free tools" | "You absolutely can experiment with free tools." | "The gap is integration, maintenance, and iteration. That is where internal teams get stuck." | AI-assisted development compresses build time 35-50%. | "Would it help to compare DIY total cost versus professional implementation?" |

## Timing Objections

| Objection | Acknowledge | Reframe | Evidence | Bridge |
|-----------|------------|---------|----------|--------|
| "Not right now, maybe next quarter" | "Of course. Timing has to be right." | Do not push. Maintain relationship. | — | "What would need to change for this to become a priority? I will check in then." |
| "We are in the middle of another project" | "Makes sense to not overload." | "Some clients start with a scoping call so when you are ready, we can move fast." | — | "Can we schedule a brief call for [specific future date]?" |
