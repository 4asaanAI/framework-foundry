# Anne — KB — MCA Filing Guide

> Form-by-form preparation checklists for all MCA filings applicable to Layaa AI Private Limited.

---

## ADT-1 (Auditor Appointment)

- [ ] Board resolution appointing auditor (draft by Anne, sign by Directors)
- [ ] Consent letter from auditor (Form ADT-1 attachment)
- [ ] Auditor's certificate of eligibility (Section 141)
- [ ] DSC of authorized signatory
- [ ] Filing fee: Rs.300 (for authorized capital up to Rs.1L)

---

## AOC-4 (Financial Statements)

- [ ] Audited Balance Sheet
- [ ] Audited Profit & Loss Account
- [ ] Cash Flow Statement
- [ ] Notes to Accounts
- [ ] Auditor's Report
- [ ] Board's Report
- [ ] DSC of Director, CFO (if applicable), and Company Secretary (if applicable)
- [ ] Filing fee: Based on authorized capital

---

## MGT-7/MGT-7A (Annual Return)

- [ ] Registered office details
- [ ] Principal business activities (NIC code: 62099)
- [ ] Particulars of holding, subsidiary, and associate companies (N/A currently)
- [ ] Share capital, debentures, and other securities
- [ ] Membership and debenture details
- [ ] Promoters, directors, and key managerial personnel
- [ ] Meeting details (Board and General Meetings)
- [ ] Remuneration details
- [ ] DSC of Director and Company Secretary (if applicable)
- [ ] Filing fee: Based on authorized capital

---

## DIR-3 KYC (Director KYC)

- [ ] Director's PAN
- [ ] Director's Aadhaar (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Director's current address proof
- [ ] Director's mobile number and email (for OTP verification)
- [ ] DSC of each director
- [ ] Filing fee: NIL (if filed before deadline); Rs.5,000 (if late)

---

## DPT-3 (Return of Deposits)

- [ ] Details of outstanding deposits (if any)
- [ ] Details of outstanding loans (classified as deposits, if applicable)
- [ ] Auditor's certificate (if deposits outstanding)
- [ ] DSC of authorized signatory
- [ ] Even if NIL — verify if filing required for private limited companies
