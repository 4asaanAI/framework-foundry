# Anne — KB — DPIIT & UDYAM Guide

> Reference document for maintaining DPIIT Startup Recognition and Udyam MSME Registration, plus Startup India benefits action items.

---

## DPIIT Startup Recognition — Maintenance

| Requirement | Frequency | Notes |
|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Valid until December 2035 | One-time recognition | No renewal needed; maintain compliance |
| Annual compliance | Annual | Ensure company filings are current (MCA, IT returns) |
| Entity should remain a startup | Continuous | Turnover must not exceed Rs.100 Cr in any FY |
| Innovation/scalability criterion | One-time verified | Already certified at recognition |

---

## Udyam Registration — Maintenance

| Requirement | Frequency | Notes |
|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Update annually on Udyam portal | Annual (around March) | Update turnover and investment figures |
| Classification maintenance | Continuous | Currently Micro Enterprise — update if investment or turnover changes category |
| PAN/Aadhaar linkage | One-time | Ensure linked correctly |

---

## Startup India Benefits — Action Items

| Benefit | Status | Action Required |
|---------|--------|-----------------|
| Section 80-IAC Tax Exemption | Not yet applied | Apply after Inter-Ministerial Board certification |
| Self-certification (labor + environment) | Available | Use when required for compliance |
| IPR Benefits | Available | File through Startup India IP facilitation when needed |
| SISFS Eligibility | Active | Grant application under preparation (see Startup Certifications & Grants Tracker) |
| Government e-Marketplace | Available | Register when responding to government tenders |
