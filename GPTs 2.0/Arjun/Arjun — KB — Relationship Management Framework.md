# Arjun — KB — Relationship Management Framework

> Client health scoring system, proactive touchpoint triggers, and communication templates for ongoing relationship management.

---

## Client Health Score

Track these signals for every active client:

| Signal | Weight | Scoring |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Response time (how fast they reply) | 15% | Fast = healthy, Slow = at risk |
| Meeting attendance | 15% | Full attendance = healthy, Cancellations = at risk |
| Feedback quality | 20% | Constructive = healthy, Complaints = at risk, Silence = warning |
| Scope alignment | 20% | On track = healthy, Creep = at risk |
| Payment behavior | 15% | On time = healthy, Late = at risk |
| Referral signals | 15% | Mentions others = healthy, No mention = neutral |

**Score Interpretation:**
- 80-100%: Healthy — maintain cadence
- 60-79%: Attention needed — increase check-ins, address concerns
- Below 60%: At risk — escalate to Founders, schedule urgent review

## Proactive Touchpoints

| Trigger | Action | Timing |
|---------|--------|--------|
| No contact for 2 weeks | Check-in email or WhatsApp | Within 24 hours of noticing |
| Milestone approaching | Pre-brief the client | 3 days before milestone |
| Delivery completed | Satisfaction check | Within 48 hours |
| Industry news relevant to client | Share with brief context note | Same day as news |
| Quarterly review due | Schedule review meeting | 2 weeks before quarter end |
| Contract renewal approaching | Begin renewal conversation | 30 days before expiry |

## Client Communication Templates

### Initial Outreach Response

```
Subject: Re: [Their inquiry topic]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out — I'd love to learn more about what you're looking for.

Before we jump into solutions, I'd like to understand your world first. Could we set up a 30-minute call this week? I'd want to understand:
- What your team looks like and how you operate today
- Where the biggest pain points are
- What you've tried before (if anything)

No pitch, no pressure — just a conversation to see if there's a fit.

Would [Day 1] or [Day 2] work for you?

Best,
[Layaa AI Signature]
```

[Layaa AI Signature]
