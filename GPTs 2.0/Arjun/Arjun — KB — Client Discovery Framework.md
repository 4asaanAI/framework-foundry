# Arjun — KB — Client Discovery Framework

> Pre-call research methodology, discovery call agenda template, and needs analysis framework for all client engagements.

---

## Pre-Call Research Checklist

Before any client interaction, complete this research:

**Company Intelligence:**
- [ ] Website review (product/service, positioning, pricing, team page)
- [ ] LinkedIn company page (size, growth trajectory, recent posts, culture signals)
- [ ] Crunchbase/AngelList (funding, investors, valuation signals)
- [ ] G2/Capterra reviews (if applicable — customer sentiment, product strengths/weaknesses)
- [ ] Job postings (tells you tech stack, where they're growing, current gaps)
- [ ] Recent news or press releases (pivots, expansions, challenges)
- [ ] Glassdoor (optional — culture and operational signals)

**Stakeholder Intelligence:**
- [ ] Primary contact LinkedIn profile (background, experience, interests, recent posts)
- [ ] Decision-maker identified (if different from contact)
- [ ] Mutual connections (warm introductions possible?)
- [ ] Communication style signals (formal LinkedIn posts = formal client; casual = casual)

**Industry Context:**
- [ ] Common pain points for this industry and size (reference ICP profiles)
- [ ] Regulatory or compliance landscape
- [ ] Competitive dynamics (who else serves this market?)
- [ ] Technology adoption trends in this sector

**Output:** A 1-page Pre-Call Brief with: company summary, stakeholder profiles, likely pain points, questions to ask, opportunities to explore, red flags to watch for.

## Discovery Call Agenda Template

```
DISCOVERY CALL AGENDA: [Client Name]
Date: [Date] | Duration: 45 minutes
Attendees: [List all attendees with roles]

1. OPENING (5 min)
   - Introductions
   - "Here's what we'd like to cover today..."
   - Set expectations: "This is about understanding your world, not pitching ours."

2. THEIR WORLD (15 min)
   - "Walk me through your business — what do you do, who are your customers?"
   - "How is your team structured? Who does what?"
   - "What tools and systems do you currently use day-to-day?"
   - "What's working well? What's not?"

3. PAIN DEEP-DIVE (15 min)
   - "You mentioned [pain point from pre-research]. Tell me more about that."
   - "Walk me through that process step by step — what happens, who's involved?"
   - "How much time does this take? How often does it happen?"
   - "What have you tried to fix this? What happened?"
   - "If you could wave a magic wand, what would change?"

4. OPPORTUNITY EXPLORATION (5 min)
   - "Based on what you've shared, here's a pattern I've seen..."
   - Test 1-2 ideas: "Would it help if [X]? How would that change things?"
   - Gauge interest and alignment

5. NEXT STEPS (5 min)
   - "Here's what I'd recommend as next steps..."
   - Define follow-up actions for both sides
   - Set timeline for next conversation
   - "Is there anyone else we should include next time?"
```
