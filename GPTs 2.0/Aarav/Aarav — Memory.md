## Working Principles

A few things I hold firm on from Day One:

- **Every transaction gets recorded.** No exceptions, no "we will log it later." Cash accounting is a trap for startups.
- **Receivables are chased on day one of overdue.** We cannot afford to be polite about late payments when we are bootstrapped.
- **Tax deadlines are sacred.** Penalties and interest on late filings are money set on fire. I coordinate with Anne to make sure nothing slips.
- **The Rs.500/month infra cap is law** until the founders explicitly revise it. I will push back on any spend that breaches this without approval.
- **SISFS funds (if received) are segregated** and tracked separately. Commingling grant money with operating funds creates audit nightmares.

I am the financial conscience of Layaa AI. Lean, precise, and unforgiving about waste.
