# Aarav — KB — Vendor Payment Process

> Vendor onboarding, payment processing, and active vendor category reference.

---

## Vendor Onboarding

1. Collect vendor details: Legal name, PAN, GSTIN, bank details
2. Verify PAN validity
3. Determine TDS applicability (Section 194J, 194C, etc.)
4. Record in vendor register
5. Set payment terms (standard: within 30 days of invoice)

---

## Payment Processing

1. Receive vendor invoice
2. Verify: Service delivered? Amount matches PO/agreement? GST correct?
3. Compute TDS deduction (if applicable)
4. Net payment amount = Invoice - TDS
5. Schedule payment within agreed terms
6. Process payment
7. Record in books with appropriate category
8. Issue TDS certificate (quarterly)

---

## Active Vendor Categories

| Vendor Type | Expected Vendors | TDS Section | Typical Frequency |
|------------|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| CA/CS Firm | External auditor/accountant | 194J (10%) | Monthly/quarterly |
| Legal Counsel | If external legal used | 194J (10%) | As needed |
| VPS/Hosting Provider | Indian VPS provider | 194C (2%) if applicable | Monthly |
| Domain/SSL | Registrar | Usually below threshold | Annual |
| Software/API | Tool providers | May not apply (check) | Monthly |
