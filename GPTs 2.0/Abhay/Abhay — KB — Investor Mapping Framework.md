# Abhay — Investor Mapping Framework

> This document contains Layaa AI's investor readiness checklist, document pack requirements, and due diligence support guidelines. This is Abhay's reference for preparing legal infrastructure for fundraising.

---

## Investor Readiness Checklist (Pre-Fundraise)

- [ ] CIN and incorporation documents in order
- [ ] DPIIT recognition certificate current
- [ ] Shareholders' Agreement executed
- [ ] Cap table clean and documented
- [ ] Financial statements audited (or compiled if pre-revenue)
- [ ] IP ownership documented (all Layaa AI IP owned by the company, not founders)
- [ ] Key contracts (client MSAs) in good standing
- [ ] No outstanding litigation or regulatory actions
- [ ] Data privacy policy and compliance documented
- [ ] Board resolutions for all material actions on file

---

## Investor Document Pack

1. **Term Sheet** — Key terms, valuation, investment amount, instrument type
2. **Shareholders' Agreement (SHA)** — Rights, obligations, transfer restrictions, anti-dilution, liquidation preference
3. **Share Subscription Agreement (SSA)** — Mechanics of share issuance
4. **Disclosure Letter** — Exceptions to warranties/representations
5. **Board Resolution** — Approving the fund-raise and authorizing share allotment

---

## Due Diligence Support

When investors conduct due diligence, prepare a virtual data room with:

- **Corporate documents:** CoI, MOA, AOA, SHA, board resolutions
- **Financial records:** Audited financials, tax returns, bank statements
- **Client contracts:** Redacted as needed
- **IP documentation:** Trademark filings, domain ownership
- **Regulatory registrations:** DPIIT, Udyam, PAN, TAN, GST
- **Employment/contractor agreements:** Currently N/A — AI workforce
- **Litigation history:** Currently nil
