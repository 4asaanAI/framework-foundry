## Data Handling Policy

```
LAYAA AI PRIVATE LIMITED — DATA HANDLING POLICY
Version: [X.X] | Effective: [Date] | Owner: Preeti (Regulatory Compliance)

1. PURPOSE
   This policy governs how Layaa AI collects, processes, stores, and
   disposes of personal and business data in compliance with DPDP Act
   2023 and IT Act 2000.

2. SCOPE
   Applies to all data processed by Layaa AI, including:
   - Internal operations data
   - Client project data (processed on behalf of clients)
   - Product user data (EduFlow, CA AI Agent)
   - Layaa OS operational data

3. DATA CLASSIFICATION
   [Reference: Data Classification & Governance Guide]

4. COLLECTION PRINCIPLES
   - Collect minimum data necessary for stated purpose
   - Obtain valid consent (explicit for personal data, parental for children)
   - Provide clear privacy notice at point of collection

5. PROCESSING RULES
   - Process only for the purpose for which data was collected
   - Maintain accuracy — correct errors within [X] days of discovery
   - Restrict access to authorized personnel/agents only

6. STORAGE & RETENTION
   - All data stored on Indian infrastructure (Indian VPS)
   - Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
   - Retention schedule:
     - Client project data: Duration of engagement + 1 year
     - Financial records: 8 years (Income Tax Act requirement)
     - User account data: Until account deletion + 30 days
     - System logs: 180 days (CERT-In requirement)
     - Marketing consent: Until consent withdrawal

7. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS
   - Right to access personal data
   - Right to correction of inaccurate data
   - Right to erasure (subject to legal retention requirements)
   - Right to grievance redressal
   - Response timeline: 30 days from receipt of request

8. BREACH RESPONSE
   [Reference incident response procedure]

9. CROSS-BORDER TRANSFERS
   - Default: No cross-border transfer
   - Exception: Only with documented assessment and Founder approval

10. REVIEW & UPDATE
    - Annual review or upon regulatory change
    - Updates approved by Founders
```

## BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) Policy

```
LAYAA AI PRIVATE LIMITED — BYOD POLICY
(Applicable when human employees/contractors are onboarded)

1. SCOPE: Personal devices used for Layaa AI work
2. PERMITTED DEVICES: Laptops, smartphones, tablets
3. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS:
   - Device encryption enabled
   - Screen lock with biometric or PIN (6+ digits)
   - Up-to-date operating system and security patches
   - No jailbroken/rooted devices
4. DATA RULES:
   - Client data must not be stored locally on personal devices
   - Access only through Layaa OS web interface (HTTPS)
   - No screenshots of client data without authorization
5. INCIDENT REPORTING: Report lost/stolen devices within 2 hours
6. REMOTE WIPE: Layaa AI reserves right to remote wipe company data
7. SEPARATION: Upon end of engagement, all company data removed
```
