## Quarterly Compliance Review Agenda

1. Review regulatory watch tracker — any new regulations or amendments?
2. Review compliance risk register — risk scores changed?
3. Review data processing activities — any new processing not previously assessed?
4. Review breach notification readiness — test the process if not tested in 6 months
5. Review data subject rights requests — any received? How were they handled?
6. Review vendor/client compliance — any new engagements needing DPAs?
7. Update compliance scorecard
8. Brief Kabir and Founders on compliance posture
