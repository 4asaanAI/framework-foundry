## Stage Definitions & Metrics

| Stage | Entry Criteria | Exit Criteria | Target Duration | Owner |
|-------|---------------|---------------|-----------------|-------|
| **Raw Lead** | Identified via inbound/outbound | Meets basic ICP fit | 1-3 days | Marketing |
| **MQL** | ICP fit confirmed, engagement signal | Discovery call scheduled | 5-10 days | Marketing → Sales handoff |
| **SQL** | Discovery completed, BANT confirmed | Proposal request or disqualified | 7-14 days | Sales (Yuvaan/Arjun) |
| **Proposal Sent** | Proposal delivered to prospect | Response received (accept/negotiate/reject) | 7-21 days | Sales (Yuvaan) |
| **Negotiation** | Active terms discussion | Agreement on terms | 5-15 days | Sales + Veer (pricing) + Abhay (legal) |
| **Won (Verbal)** | Client verbally commits | Contract signed | 3-7 days | Founders |
| **Won (Paid)** | 50% advance received | Project kicks off | 1-5 days | Aarav confirms |
| **Lost** | Disqualified at any stage | Loss reason documented | — | Rishi documents |
| **Stalled** | No activity for 14+ days | Re-engaged or moved to Lost | — | Rishi flags |

## Conversion Rate Benchmarks

| Transition | Target | Yellow Flag | Red Flag | Diagnosis |
|-----------|--------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| MQL → SQL | 25% | 15-24% | Below 15% | Lead quality issue. Check ICP targeting with @Mira |
| SQL → Proposal | 60% | 40-59% | Below 40% | Discovery or qualification gap. Check with @Yuvaan |
| Proposal → Won | 35% | 25-34% | Below 25% | Pricing or value gap. Check with @Veer |
| End-to-End (MQL → Won) | 5-8% | 3-5% | Below 3% | Systemic pipeline failure. Escalate to @Kabir |
| Total Sales Cycle | 42-65 days | 65-120 days | Above 120 days | Process drag. Identify stuck stage |
