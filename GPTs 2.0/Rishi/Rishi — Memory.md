## FORECASTING METHODOLOGY DECISIONS

### Three-Scenario Model (Adopted)
The forecasting approach uses three scenarios:
- **Conservative:** Only deals at Proposal stage or later. Probability-weighted (Proposal 35%, Negotiation 60%, Won Verbal 85%).
- **Expected:** All active pipeline from SQL stage onward. Stage-weighted probabilities.
- **Optimistic:** All pipeline from MQL onward. Includes early-stage deals with strong signals.

### Forecast Accuracy
- No forecast has been produced yet. The model is defined but has never been run against real data.
- Once the first forecast is produced, accuracy will be tracked monthly. Variance thresholds: within 15% is green, 15-30% is yellow, above 30% is red.

### Forecasting Limitations at Current Scale
- With fewer than 5 active deals, a formal weighted-probability forecast adds no meaningful value beyond "we have X deals at Y stages."
- **Decision made:** Rishi will produce narrative pipeline snapshots (not probabilistic forecasts) until pipeline reaches 10+ active deals. At that point, switch to the three-scenario model.

## BOTTLENECKS IDENTIFIED

### 1. Pipeline is Too Small
- The most critical revenue operations bottleneck. There are not enough deals in the pipeline to generate reliable conversion data, meaningful forecasts, or diversified revenue.
- **Root cause:** All lead generation is founder-driven. No marketing engine exists yet.
- **Remedy:** First formal marketing campaigns (via Mira and Nia) need to generate MQLs that flow through a tracked pipeline.

### 2. Revenue Concentration — Education-Heavy
- Majority of current revenue comes from 2-3 school clients. If one relationship sours, revenue impact is outsized.
- **Risk level:** High at current scale. Acceptable for an early-stage company but must be actively diversified.
- **Remedy:** CA AI Agent launch will open a second revenue vertical. Additional school clients from referral pipeline will reduce single-client concentration.

### 3. No CRM or Pipeline Tracking Tool
- Deals are not tracked in a structured system. This means no stage durations, no stall detection, no automated follow-up reminders, no conversion rate calculations.
- **Impact:** Cannot run pipeline health indicators, cannot produce data-backed forecasts, cannot identify where deals are getting stuck.
- **Remedy:** CRM selection and configuration is a prerequisite for Rishi to operate at full capability. Must coordinate with Nia (Campaign & Funnel Execution Coordinator) on tool selection.

### 4. No Sales Process Standardization
- Current sales follows founder intuition, not a repeatable process. There is no standard discovery call structure, no qualification checklist consistently applied, no systematic follow-up cadence.
- **Impact:** Cannot diagnose where in the process deals are being lost or delayed.
- **Remedy:** Yuvaan (Sales Enablement Specialist) has defined sales SOPs in the Knowledge Base. These need to be adopted in practice.
