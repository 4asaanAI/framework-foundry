**Last Updated:** April 2026
**Status:** Living Document — Major changes require Founder approval

---

## Briefing Tara (Brand Voice & Content Architect)

When you need content created, provide:
1. **Strategic context:** Why this content, why now, which campaign or GTM objective
2. **Target audience:** Specific segment and persona
3. **Key message:** What the audience should think/feel/do after consuming this
4. **Content pillar:** P1-P5 reference
5. **Channel:** Where this will be published (determines format and tone)
6. **Founder attribution:** Whether this should be ghostwritten for a specific founder
7. **Evidence requirements:** What claims need validation from Kshitiz
8. **Deadline:** When the draft is needed

## Briefing Zoya (Performance Marketing & Growth Architect)

When you need channel strategy, provide:
1. **Campaign objective:** What business outcome you want
2. **Target segment:** ICP definition and firmographics
3. **Budget range:** Approved or proposed budget
4. **Timeline:** Campaign duration and key dates
5. **Messaging direction:** Key messages (Tara handles actual copy)
6. **Success metrics:** What constitutes success for this campaign
7. **Constraints:** Budget limits, channel restrictions, compliance requirements

## Coordinating with Nia (Campaign & Funnel Execution Coordinator)

When handing off for execution:
1. **Campaign brief:** Complete, approved campaign brief (see Campaign Playbooks & Content Strategy KB)
2. **Execution sequence:** What happens in what order
3. **Tracking requirements:** UTM standards, KPIs, reporting cadence
4. **Escalation triggers:** When Nia should flag issues back to you
5. **Dependencies:** What needs to be ready from Tara, Zoya, or other agents

## Requesting Data from Rishi (Revenue Operations Strategist)

When you need pipeline or revenue data:
1. **Specific data needed:** Which metrics, which time period, which segments
2. **Purpose:** What decision this data will inform
3. **Format:** Summary or detailed breakdown
4. **Urgency:** When you need it

## Requesting Research from Kshitiz (Master Research & Data Analyst)

Use the Research Request Template (see Market Research Framework KB). Always specify:
- What you need validated vs. what you need researched from scratch
- The evidence standard required ([VALIDATED] vs. [PENDING] acceptable)
- The deadline and priority

*These protocols ensure structured, efficient communication between Mira and all collaborating agents within the Layaa AI workforce. Follow these briefing structures for every inter-agent request.*
