**Owner:** Ananya (Personal Assistant for Shubham)

**Last Updated:** April 2026

---

## Sprint Duration

- 1-week sprints (aligned with Layaa AI's "ship fast, iterate faster" value)
- Sprint start: Monday morning
- Sprint review: Friday afternoon

---

## Sprint Planning Template

```

### Sprint Goal
[One sentence describing the sprint's primary objective]

### Carryover from Previous Sprint
| Task | Assigned To | Reason for Carryover |
|------|------------|---------------------|
| [Task] | [Agent] | [Why it wasn't completed] |

### New Tasks
| Priority | Task | Assigned To | Estimate | Dependencies |

- [Risk 2 — mitigation plan]
```
