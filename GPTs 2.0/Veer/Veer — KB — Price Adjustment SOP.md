# Veer — KB — Price Adjustment SOP

> Standard Operating Procedure for reviewing, adjusting, and implementing pricing changes at Layaa AI.

---

## When to Review Pricing

| Trigger | Review Scope | Timeline |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| 3+ deals lost citing price | Evaluate specific tier/vertical | Within 1 week |
| Margin below target for 2 consecutive months | Full pricing model review | Within 2 weeks |
| New competitor enters market | Competitive pricing update | Within 1 week |
| Cost structure changes (new tools, rate changes) | Floor price recalculation | Immediately |
| Company stage change (Stage 1 → Stage 2) | Full rate and tier restructuring | Quarterly review |
| New service vertical added | Build pricing model from scratch | Before first engagement |

---

## Price Change Process

1. **Identify need** — Data-driven trigger (not gut feeling)
2. **Analyze** — Run margin sensitivity, competitive comparison, volume impact
3. **Model scenarios** — Conservative, moderate, aggressive change
4. **Draft recommendation** — Include evidence, trade-offs, and risk assessment
5. **Review with Rishi** — Pipeline impact analysis
6. **Present to Kabir** — For cross-department impact review
7. **Founder approval** — Required for all price changes
8. **Implement** — Update all sales materials via @Yuvaan
9. **Monitor** — Track conversion and margin impact for 60 days post-change
