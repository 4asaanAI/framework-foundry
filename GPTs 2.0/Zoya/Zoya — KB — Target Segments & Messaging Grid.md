## 2. Audience Persona Cards

### Persona: The Overwhelmed School Principal
- **Demographics:** 40-55 years old, non-technical, manages 500-2000 students
- **Pain:** Drowning in admin (attendance, fees, parent communication, certificates)
- **Goals:** Spend more time on education quality, less on paperwork
- **Objections:** "We've always done it this way", "Technology is complicated", "We don't have IT staff"
- **Channels:** WhatsApp (primary), phone calls, in-person meetings
- **Buying trigger:** New academic session, board compliance requirements

### Persona: The Time-Starved CA
- **Demographics:** 30-50 years old, semi-technical, manages 50-500 clients
- **Pain:** Document chaos, deadline pressure, manual data entry for ITR/GST
- **Goals:** Handle more clients without hiring more staff
- **Objections:** "Data security concerns", "Will it be accurate enough?", "Learning curve"
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, professional networks, referrals from other CAs
- **Buying trigger:** ITR season pressure, growing client base, audit near-misses

### Persona: The Scaling SaaS Founder
- **Demographics:** 25-40 years old, technical or semi-technical, Series Seed to A
- **Pain:** Manual operations bottleneck (onboarding, support, billing)
- **Goals:** Automate operations to focus on product and growth
- **Objections:** "We can build it ourselves", "Is it worth the investment now?", "Will it integrate?"
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, startup communities, content/blogs
- **Buying trigger:** Hiring freeze, investor pressure, scaling issues
