## 1. Growth Strategy Canvas

Use this template for any new growth initiative:

```
GROWTH STRATEGY: [Initiative Name]
Date: [Date]
Owner: Zoya (Strategy) -> Nia (Execution)
Status: [Draft / Reviewed by Mira / Approved / Active]

OBJECTIVE:
- Business Goal: [e.g., Generate 30 MQLs from CA firms in Q3]
- Growth Metric: [Primary metric — MQLs, signups, demos booked]
- Timeline: [Duration]

TARGET SEGMENT:
- ICP: [Specific segment from Mira's definitions]
- Firmographics: [Size, industry, location, revenue]
- Decision Maker: [Title, role, pain points]
- Behavioral Triggers: [What signals buying readiness]

CHANNEL STRATEGY:
| Channel | Role in Funnel | Budget | Expected Output | CAC Target |
|---------|---------------|--------|-----------------|-----------|
| [Channel] | [Awareness/Consideration/Conversion] | Rs.[X] | [MQLs/Clicks/Leads] | Rs.[X] |

GROWTH MECHANICS:
- Primary Loop: [Describe the growth mechanism]
- Compounding Factor: [What makes this grow over time]
- Bottleneck: [Biggest risk to growth]

FUNNEL ARCHITECTURE:
Awareness -> [Touchpoint] -> Interest -> [Touchpoint] -> Consideration -> [Touchpoint] -> Action -> [Touchpoint] -> Retention -> [Touchpoint] -> Referral

TESTING PLAN:
- Hypothesis: [What we believe will work]
- Minimum Viable Test: [Smallest test to validate]
- Data Required: [How many impressions/clicks/leads before we decide]
- Decision Criteria: [What number = proceed, what = pivot]

SUCCESS METRICS:
| Metric | Target | Red Flag | Kill Criteria |
|--------|--------|----------|---------------|
| [Metric] | [Target] | [Warning] | [Stop if...] |

EVIDENCE STATUS:
- [All data points tagged]

ESCALATION:
- Escalate to Mira if: [Triggers]
- Escalate to Kabir if: [Triggers]
```
