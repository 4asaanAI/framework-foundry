## 1. Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) Playbook

**Best For:** Brand awareness, retargeting, visual content promotion, reaching SME decision-makers who are active on social media outside work hours

**Targeting Options for Layaa AI:**
- **Interest-based:** Small business owners, automation, AI tools, business management
- **Behavior-based:** Business page admins, digital activity signals
- **Lookalike audiences:** From existing client list (when sufficient data exists)
- **Custom audiences:** Website visitors, email list, engagement audiences
- **Geographic:** Gurgaon, Delhi NCR initially; Tier 1 cities in Phase 2

**Ad Format Priorities:**
1. Video ads (Reels format) — highest reach, lowest CPM
2. Carousel ads — educational content, multi-benefit presentation
3. Single image ads — direct response, clear CTA
4. Lead form ads — in-platform lead capture for lower-friction conversion

**Budget Guidance:**
- Minimum viable test: Rs.5,000 for 7 days
- Learning phase: Platform needs ~50 conversions per ad set per week for optimization
- At small budgets: Consolidate ad sets, do not fragment
- Bidding: Start with lowest-cost bidding, shift to cost-cap once baseline established

**KPI Benchmarks (India B2B — Estimated):**
| Metric | Good | Average | Poor |
|--------|------|---------|------|
| CPM | <Rs.100 | Rs.100-200 | >Rs.200 |
| CTR | >1.5% | 0.8-1.5% | <0.8% |
| CPC | <Rs.15 | Rs.15-40 | >Rs.40 |
| Lead Form Completion | >20% | 10-20% | <10% |

## 2. LinkedIn Ads Playbook

**Best For:** Reaching SME decision-makers by title, company size, industry; high-intent professional audience; thought leadership amplification

**Targeting Options for Layaa AI:**
- **Title-based:** CEO, Founder, Managing Director, COO, Head of Operations, Principal (schools)
- **Company size:** 1-50, 51-200 employees
- **Industry:** Education, Financial Services, IT Services, Professional Services, Logistics
- **Geography:** India (NCR initially)
- **Seniority:** Director, VP, CXO, Owner
- **Skills:** Automation, AI, Business Operations

**Ad Format Priorities:**
1. Sponsored Content (single image) — thought leadership amplification
2. Message Ads (InMail) — direct outreach to specific decision-makers
3. Document Ads — educational content distribution (frameworks, guides)
4. Conversation Ads — multi-path engagement
5. Lead Gen Forms — in-platform capture

**Budget Guidance:**
- LinkedIn is expensive: Rs.300-500+ per click for India B2B
- Minimum viable test: Rs.10,000 for 14 days
- Focus on high-intent formats (Message Ads, Lead Gen Forms) at small budgets
- Do not use LinkedIn for broad awareness at small budgets — use for precision targeting only

## 3. Google Ads Playbook

**Best For:** Capturing existing search intent; reaching people actively searching for AI automation, school management software, or CA practice tools

**Keyword Strategy for Layaa AI:**
- **High Intent:** "AI automation for SME", "school management software India", "CA practice management", "automation consulting India"
- **Medium Intent:** "how to automate business processes", "AI for small business", "school ERP India", "CA software with AI"
- **Low Intent / Educational:** "what is AI automation", "benefits of automation for business", "AI in education India"
- **Negative Keywords:** "free", "tutorial", "course", "jobs", "salary", "enterprise"

**Campaign Structure:**
1. Search campaigns (high-intent keywords) — primary
2. Performance Max (when enough conversion data exists)
3. Display retargeting (for website visitors)
4. YouTube ads (for educational content, when video assets exist)

**Budget Guidance:**
- Minimum viable test: Rs.5,000 for 14 days
- Start with exact match and phrase match only — avoid broad match at small budgets
- Focus budget on highest-intent keywords first
- Enable conversion tracking before spending any money

## 4. Email Marketing Playbook

**Best For:** Nurturing leads, onboarding clients, re-engaging dormant prospects, delivering educational content to warm audience

**Email Types:**
1. **Nurture sequences** — 5-7 emails over 14-21 days for new leads
2. **Onboarding sequences** — 5 emails over 14 days for new clients
3. **Newsletter** — Weekly or bi-weekly value-driven email
4. **Campaign-specific** — Event invitations, product launches, special offers
5. **Re-engagement** — 3-4 emails for inactive leads

**Best Practices:**
- Subject lines: 6-8 words, benefit-focused, no clickbait
- Send time: 10 AM IST Tuesday-Thursday (India B2B best practice)
- Frequency: No more than 2 emails/week to any individual
- Personalization: Name, company, industry where known
- Single CTA per email
- Mobile-optimized (60%+ of Indian email opens are mobile)
- Always include unsubscribe option
- A/B test subject lines on every campaign

**KPI Benchmarks:**
| Metric | Good | Average | Poor |
|--------|------|---------|------|
| Open Rate (Cold) | >25% | 15-25% | <15% |
| Open Rate (Warm) | >35% | 20-35% | <20% |
| CTR | >3% | 1-3% | <1% |
| Reply Rate (Cold) | >3% | 1-3% | <1% |
| Unsubscribe | <0.5% | 0.5-1% | >1% |

## 5. Organic / SEO Playbook

**Best For:** Long-term sustainable traffic, building topical authority, supporting paid campaigns with quality content, reducing CAC over time

**SEO Strategy for Layaa AI:**

**Content Clusters:**
1. AI Automation for SMEs (pillar page + 10-15 supporting articles)
2. School Management with AI (pillar page + 8-10 supporting articles)
3. AI for CA Firms (pillar page + 8-10 supporting articles)
4. Business Process Automation India (pillar page + 10-15 supporting articles)

**Technical SEO Priorities:**
- Site speed optimization (target <3s load time)
- Mobile-first indexing compliance
- Schema markup for services, FAQ, organization
- XML sitemap and robots.txt optimization
- Internal linking structure
- HTTPS (already in place)

**On-Page SEO Checklist:**
- [ ] Primary keyword in title, H1, first paragraph, meta description
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2s and body text naturally
- [ ] Meta description 150-160 characters, compelling
- [ ] Image alt text with keywords where relevant
- [ ] Internal links to related content (3-5 per article)
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources (1-2 per article)
- [ ] URL structure clean and keyword-rich

**Timeline Expectations:**
- Month 1-3: Foundation (technical SEO, pillar content)
- Month 3-6: Building (content production, link earning)
- Month 6-12: Traction (first page rankings, organic traffic growth)
- Month 12+: Compounding (organic becomes significant lead source)
