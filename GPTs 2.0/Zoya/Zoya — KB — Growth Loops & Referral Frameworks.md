## 1. Growth Loop Types

**Paid Flywheel:**
Client Revenue -> Allocate % to marketing -> Acquire new clients -> Generate revenue -> Reinvest
- Key metric: Payback period (how fast does a client's revenue cover their acquisition cost?)
- Target: <3 month payback period for retainer clients

**Content Flywheel:**
Publish content -> Attract traffic -> Convert to MQLs -> Close clients -> Get case studies -> Publish better content
- Key metric: Content ROI (leads generated per piece of content)
- Compounding factor: SEO value of content grows over time

**Referral Loop:**
Deliver excellent service -> Client refers another SME -> New client signs -> Deliver excellent service -> Repeat
- Key metric: Referral rate (% of clients who refer at least one other business)
- Target: 30%+ referral rate

**Community Loop:**
Share knowledge freely -> Build trust and following -> Attract inbound interest -> Convert to clients -> Share learnings
- Key metric: Inbound lead volume from organic channels
- Compounding factor: Founder personal brand compounds over time

## 2. Referral Program Framework (Proposed)

```
REFERRAL PROGRAM: [Name TBD]
Status: Proposed — requires Founder approval

MECHANICS:
- Existing clients refer another SME
- If referred SME signs an implementation deal:
  - Referrer: [Discount on next month's retainer / cash reward / extended support]
  - Referee: [Discount on implementation fee]

TRACKING:
- Unique referral codes per client
- Track in CRM with referral source field
- Monthly referral report

CONSTRAINTS:
- Referral reward must not exceed 10% of deal value (margin protection)
- Both parties must be legitimate businesses (no self-referrals)
- Program terms require Abhay (Legal) review before launch

EXPECTED IMPACT:
- Target: 2-3 referral leads per quarter from existing clients
- Expected conversion: 40-50% (warm leads convert higher)
- Cost per acquisition: Significantly below paid channel CAC
```

**Ways to increase organic spread:**

2. Case studies shared by clients
