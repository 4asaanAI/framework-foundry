## Hook Patterns (Proven Effective)

1. **Contrarian:** "Everyone says X. Here's why that's wrong for Indian SMEs."
2. **Story-led:** "Last week, a school principal told us something that changed how we think about AI."
3. **Question:** "What if your team had 10 extra hours every week?"
4. **Framework:** "3 questions every SME should ask before investing in AI automation."
5. **Hot Take:** "Most AI tools are built for companies that don't need them."
6. **BTS:** "We just shipped a feature that took 3 weeks. Here's what went wrong."
7. **List:** "5 signs your business is ready for AI automation (and 3 signs it's not)."
8. **Observation:** "I've noticed something about how Indian SMEs think about technology."
