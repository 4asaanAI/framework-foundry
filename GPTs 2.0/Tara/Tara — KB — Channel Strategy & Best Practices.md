# Tara — KB — Channel Strategy & Best Practices

## LinkedIn Best Practices

### Algorithm Signals
- First-hour engagement matters most — post 8-10 AM IST for Indian business audience
- Comments > Reactions > Shares for algorithmic boost
- Personal posts outperform company page posts
- Posts with questions or opinions generate more comments
- Avoid external links in post body (put in comments)
- Consistent frequency > sporadic high-effort posts

### Content That Works for B2B India
- Founder stories and building-in-public narratives
- Specific, actionable frameworks (numbered lists, step-by-step)
- Counter-intuitive observations about AI or business
- Industry-specific insights (schools, CA firms, SME challenges)
- Behind-the-scenes of building a company

### Content That Does NOT Work
- Generic motivational quotes
- Feature announcements without context
- Overly technical deep-dives (save for blog)
- Self-congratulatory company updates
- Repurposed marketing collateral without personal angle

## Instagram Strategy

### Content Focus: Building-in-Public
- Show the real journey of building Layaa AI
- Honest about challenges, not just wins
- First-person, founder-led
- Short, punchy, visual

### Format Priorities
1. **Reels** (highest reach) — 15-60 seconds, talking head or screen share
2. **Carousels** (highest saves) — Educational, framework-based
3. **Stories** (highest engagement with existing followers) — Daily BTS

### Instagram Voice
- More casual than LinkedIn
- More visual language
- Shorter sentences
- More personality, less formality
- Still no hype, no jargon, no banned words

## Email Copywriting

### Subject Line Best Practices
- 6-8 words maximum
- Benefit-focused or curiosity-driven
- No clickbait, no ALL CAPS, no excessive punctuation
- Personalization tokens where appropriate
- A/B test 2 subject lines per campaign

### Email Sequence Types

**Nurture (New Lead):** Welcome (Day 0) → Educational (Day 3) → Case study (Day 7) → Framework (Day 10) → Soft CTA (Day 14)

**Onboarding (New Client):** Welcome (Day 0) → Getting started (Day 1) → Quick win (Day 3) → Check-in (Day 7) → Feedback (Day 14)

**Re-engagement (Inactive):** "We noticed..." (Day 0) → New content (Day 5) → Value reminder (Day 10) → Last chance CTA (Day 15)

### Email Tone
- Personal, not corporate
- Conversational, as if writing to one person
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each)
- Single CTA per email
- Founder sign-off for high-value sequences
