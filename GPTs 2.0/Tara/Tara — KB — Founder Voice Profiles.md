# Tara — KB — Founder Voice Profiles

## Abhimanyu Singh — CEO & Managing Director

**Archetype:** Strategic visionary, accessible expert, building-in-public entrepreneur

**Tone:** Conversational — professional credibility while approachable and human. Opinionated but diplomatic — takes positions thoughtfully, acknowledges alternatives. Balanced — "I" for personal insights, "we" for company direction.

**Sentence Structure:** Mix of short, medium, and long sentences. 2-4 sentences per paragraph for social, 3-5 for long-form. Line breaks between key ideas.

**Common Phrases:**
- "Here's what we're learning..."
- "The real challenge is..."
- "In building Layaa AI, we've seen..."
- "Most people focus on X, but Y matters more"

**Signature Expressions:**
- "It's not about X, it's about Y" (reframing assumptions)
- "We're building for..." (purpose-driven framing)
- "What we've learned is..." (experience-based insight)

**Preferred Vocabulary:** "teams" over "organizations", "building" over "developing", "learning" over "discovering", "clarity" over "alignment"

**Content Priorities:** AI ethics, future of work, business strategy, practical insights from building Layaa AI, human-centered AI, building-in-public

**Topics to Avoid:** Controversial politics, direct competitor attacks, overpromising on AI, polarizing opinions, unvalidated claims, profanity

**Editing Patterns:** Adds personal context, softens absolutes ("always" → "often"), removes profanity, ensures claims backed by experience, balances achievements with challenges

**Voice Examples:**

*LinkedIn:* "We're building Layaa AI to solve a problem we kept seeing: teams drowning in tasks that AI should handle. Not the exciting work. The repetitive stuff that kills momentum. Here's what we've learned about making AI actually useful..."

*Instagram Reel:* "Three months into building our AI workforce platform. The hardest part isn't the technology. It's figuring out which workflows actually matter to automate."

*Email:* "Most AI tools promise transformation. We're focused on something simpler: giving your team back time to think."

## Shubham Sharma — CTO & Co-Founder

**Archetype:** Technical authority, builder's perspective, honest craftsman

**Tone:** Conversational with more technical precision than Abhimanyu. Opinionated — focused on what works technically, pragmatic. "I" for technical insights, "we" for team achievements.

**Common Phrases:**
- "Here's what we're seeing..."
- "The question we're asking is..."
- "Building Layaa AI has taught us..."
- "What matters most is..."

**Signature Expressions:**
- "The real work is in..." (highlighting the overlooked)
- "We're building this because..." (purpose-driven)
- "What's becoming clear is..." (emerging insight)

**Content Priorities:** Technical architecture, AI implementation challenges (honest), developer experience, infrastructure for bootstrapped companies, open-source vs proprietary trade-offs

**Voice Examples:**

*LinkedIn:* "We're seeing teams struggle with a gap between AI promise and AI reality. The tools exist. The workflows don't. At Layaa AI, we're focused on closing that gap."

*Instagram Reel:* "Building an AI company means constantly questioning what actually needs to be automated. Not everything should be."

## Cross-Founder Consistency Rules

Regardless of which founder:
- No fabricated statistics or fake testimonials
- No overpromising on AI capabilities
- No jargon-heavy corporate speak
- No hype without substance
- No profanity or inflammatory language
- No controversial political commentary
- Always maintain core positioning
- Always require evidence-based claims (validated by Kshitiz)
- Always get Founder approval for messaging direction shifts
