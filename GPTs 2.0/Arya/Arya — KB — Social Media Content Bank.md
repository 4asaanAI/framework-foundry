### Post 2 — "The 3-step AI readiness test every SME should run before buying anything" (Framework)

**Hook:**
Before you buy any AI tool, run this 3-step test. It takes 15 minutes. It could save you lakhs.

**Body:**
We've worked with enough Indian SMEs to know: the ones who succeed with AI aren't the ones with the biggest budgets. They're the ones who were ready.

Here's the test we run internally:

**Step 1: The Process Inventory (5 min)**
List every task your team does that follows the same steps every time. Data entry. Follow-up emails. Report generation. Invoice processing. If a new hire could learn it from a checklist, it's a candidate.

**Step 2: The Volume Check (5 min)**
For each task, estimate: How many times per week? How many minutes each time? Multiply. If a task takes 30 minutes and happens 10 times a week, that's 5 hours. That's your automation ROI right there.

**Step 3: The Dependency Test (5 min)**
Ask: Does this task need human judgment at every step, or only at the start and end? If your team makes the same decision 95% of the time, the "judgment" is actually a rule — and rules can be automated.

If you pass all three steps for even one process, you're AI-ready. Not because the technology is ready — it's been ready. Because your team knows exactly what to automate and why.

**CTA:**

### Post 7 — "The automation ROI formula we use at Layaa AI" (Framework)

**Hook:**
Every client asks: "What's the ROI of automation?" Here's the exact formula we use.

**Body:**
We call it the **3T Formula: Time, Transactions, Trust.**

**Time Saved:**
Hours per week spent on the task x Team member's effective hourly cost = Weekly savings.
Example: 10 hours/week x Rs.250/hour = Rs.2,500/week = Rs.10,000/month.

**Transactions Recovered:**
Revenue lost to slow response, missed follow-ups, or dropped leads.
Example: If you respond to inquiries 4 hours faster, your conversion rate improves by [X]%. On 50 monthly leads worth Rs.20K each, even a 5% improvement = Rs.50K/month.

**Trust Built:**
This one's harder to measure but impossible to ignore. When your client gets an instant acknowledgment, a same-day proposal, a weekly update without being asked — they trust you more. They refer you more. They renew faster.

The automation implementation costs Rs.2-3 lakhs. If the monthly savings exceed Rs.15-20K, payback is under 6 months. Most of our clients see payback in 3-4 months.

The businesses that struggle with ROI are the ones automating low-volume tasks. The ones that succeed automate high-frequency, time-sensitive processes where speed directly impacts revenue.
