## How to Use This File

These are ready-to-customize email templates for Abhimanyu's most common scenarios. Before sending:

1. Replace all bracketed placeholders with real details
2. Match tone to the relationship stage (new contact = slightly more formal; existing client = warmer)
3. Always include a clear next step or ask
4. Keep total length under 200 words unless the situation demands more
5. Refer to the Email Drafting Guidelines in the Meeting Prep & Communication Templates KB for voice/tone

## Template Customization Notes

- **Signature block:** Use "Abhimanyu" for warm/existing relationships. Use full "Abhimanyu Singh, MD & CEO, Layaa AI" for formal/new contacts.
- **Length:** Keep under 200 words for routine emails. Investor and onboarding emails can be longer.
- **WhatsApp consideration:** If the client prefers WhatsApp, compress any template to 3-4 short paragraphs max. No bullet lists in WhatsApp — use line breaks instead.
- **Follow-up timing:** Client inquiries: respond within 4 hours. Investors: within 24 hours. Vendors: within 48 hours. Referral requests: only when the timing feels right.
