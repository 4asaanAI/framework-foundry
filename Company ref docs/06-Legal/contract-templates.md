## 1. Purpose

This document provides the structural framework for all contracts that Layaa AI Private Limited enters into. It defines key sections, standard terms, and Layaa AI-specific clauses for each contract type. These templates serve as starting points -- every contract should be reviewed for deal-specific customisation before execution.

**Important:** These templates are not a substitute for legal advice. For contracts above INR 5 Lakh in value or involving cross-border parties, obtain review from a qualified legal professional.

## 2. Standard Clauses (Common to All Contracts)

The following clauses appear in every Layaa AI contract. Specific language for each is provided in the Clause Library (separate document).

### 2.1 Arbitration Clause

```
Any dispute, controversy, or claim arising out of or in connection with this Agreement,
including any question regarding its existence, validity, or termination, shall be
referred to and finally resolved by arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and
Conciliation Act, 1996, as amended from time to time.

Seat of Arbitration: Gurgaon, Haryana, India
Language of Arbitration: English
Number of Arbitrators: One (1), mutually appointed. If parties fail to agree within
30 days, the arbitrator shall be appointed in accordance with the Act.
```

### 2.2 Jurisdiction Clause

```
Subject to the arbitration clause above, the courts in Gurgaon, Haryana shall have
exclusive jurisdiction over any matters not subject to arbitration.
```

### 2.3 Liability Cap

```
The total aggregate liability of Layaa AI under this Agreement shall not exceed the
total fees paid or payable by the Client under this Agreement (or the relevant
Statement of Work, if applicable) in the twelve (12) months preceding the event
giving rise to such liability.

In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental, consequential,
special, or punitive damages, including loss of profits, loss of data, or loss of
business opportunity, regardless of the cause of action or the theory of liability.
```

### 2.4 IP Assignment

Default position (customise per deal):

```
All Intellectual Property created specifically for the Client under this Agreement
("Client IP") shall be assigned to the Client upon receipt of full payment for the
relevant deliverables.

All pre-existing IP of Layaa AI, including but not limited to frameworks, libraries,
tools, methodologies, and general-purpose code ("Layaa AI Background IP"), shall
remain the exclusive property of Layaa AI. The Client is granted a non-exclusive,
perpetual, royalty-free licence to use Layaa AI Background IP solely to the extent
embedded in the deliverables.

Any improvements, enhancements, or derivative works to Layaa AI Background IP created
during the engagement shall remain the property of Layaa AI.
```

### 2.5 Payment Terms

```
Standard Payment Terms:
- Invoices due within 15 days of invoice date ("Net 15").
- Late payment interest: 1.5% per month on the outstanding amount.
- Layaa AI reserves the right to suspend services if payment is overdue by more
  than 30 days.
- All amounts are exclusive of GST (18%), which shall be added to invoices as
  applicable under law.
```

## 3. Master Service Agreement (MSA)

### 3.1 Purpose

The MSA governs the overall relationship between Layaa AI and a client. Individual engagements are executed through Statements of Work (SoW) under the MSA.

### 3.2 Structure

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **1. Definitions** | Key terms: "Services", "Deliverables", "Confidential Information", "Intellectual Property", "Client Data", "Effective Date", "Term" |
| **2. Scope of Agreement** | MSA governs relationship; individual projects via SoW. MSA prevails in case of conflict unless SoW explicitly overrides specific MSA terms. |
| **3. Engagement Model** | How SoWs are initiated, approved, and executed. Change order process. |
| **4. Fees and Payment** | General payment framework (specific amounts in SoW). Standard payment terms (Net 15). Late payment consequences. GST treatment. |
| **5. Intellectual Property** | Default IP assignment framework (see Section 2.4 above). Customisation mechanism via SoW. |
| **6. Confidentiality** | Mutual NDA-equivalent terms. Survival period: 3 years post-termination (or indefinite for trade secrets). |
| **7. Data Protection** | DPDP Act compliance. Data processing terms. Breach notification (72 hours). Reference to DPA if personal data is processed. |
| **8. Representations & Warranties** | Each party: authority to enter agreement, no conflict with other obligations, compliance with applicable law. Layaa AI: services performed with reasonable skill and care. |
| **9. Limitation of Liability** | Cap at 12 months' fees. Exclusion of indirect/consequential damages. Carve-outs: fraud, wilful misconduct, IP infringement, confidentiality breach, data breach. |
| **10. Indemnification** | Mutual indemnification for third-party claims arising from breach. IP infringement indemnity (Layaa AI indemnifies for IP claims on deliverables). |
| **11. Term and Termination** | Initial term (typically 12 months, auto-renewing). Termination for convenience (30 days' written notice). Termination for cause (material breach, 15-day cure period). Effect of termination on active SoWs. |
| **12. Termination Consequences** | Payment for work completed. Return/destruction of confidential information. Survival clauses. Transition assistance (if applicable). |
| **13. Force Majeure** | Events beyond reasonable control. Notification obligation (5 business days). Right to terminate if force majeure exceeds 60 days. |
| **14. Non-Solicitation** | Neither party shall solicit employees/contractors of the other during the term and for 12 months after. Does not restrict general recruitment advertisements. |
| **15. Dispute Resolution** | Escalation: project leads then Founders/senior management (15 days), then mediation (30 days), then arbitration per standard clause. |
| **16. General Provisions** | Entire agreement. Amendment (written, signed by both). Severability. Waiver. Assignment (not without consent). Notices. Governing law (India). Jurisdiction (Gurgaon). |

### 3.3 Layaa AI-Specific MSA Provisions

- **Founder Involvement Clause:** "Layaa AI shall ensure that at least one Founder-Director is directly involved in the oversight and quality assurance of services delivered under this Agreement."
- **Bootstrap Transparency:** "Layaa AI operates as a startup entity. The Client acknowledges that Layaa AI's team size is limited and delivery timelines are scoped accordingly."
- **Tool and Third-Party Disclosure:** "Layaa AI may use third-party tools, APIs, and AI services in delivering the Services. A list of material third-party dependencies will be disclosed in the relevant SoW."
- **DPIIT Startup Clause:** "Layaa AI is a DPIIT-recognised startup (Certificate No. DIPP245808). Applicable startup benefits under government policy may be referenced in commercial terms."

## 4. Statement of Work (SoW)

### 4.1 Purpose

Each SoW defines a specific engagement under the MSA. It contains project-specific scope, deliverables, timeline, and pricing.

### 4.2 Structure

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **1. Reference** | SoW number, effective date, reference to governing MSA |
| **2. Project Overview** | Brief description of the engagement, business context, and objectives |
| **3. Scope of Work** | Detailed description of services to be performed. Inclusions and explicit exclusions. |
| **4. Deliverables** | Itemised list of deliverables with descriptions and acceptance criteria |
| **5. Timeline & Milestones** | Project schedule, milestone dates, dependencies, and assumptions |
| **6. Acceptance Process** | How deliverables are reviewed and accepted. Review period (typically 5--10 business days). Deemed acceptance if no written objection within review period. |
| **7. Fees & Payment Schedule** | Total project fee (or rate card for T&M). Payment milestones tied to deliverables. Expense policy. |
| **8. Team & Resources** | Layaa AI team members assigned. Client-side resources and access required. |
| **9. Assumptions & Dependencies** | What Layaa AI assumes the Client will provide (data, access, decisions). Risks if assumptions are not met. |
| **10. Change Management** | Process for scope changes. Change Order form. Impact on timeline and fees. |
| **11. Project-Specific IP Terms** | If different from MSA defaults (e.g., licence-only instead of assignment, or specific background IP exclusions). |
| **12. Project-Specific Data Terms** | Types of data to be processed. Data retention and deletion schedule. Special handling requirements. |
| **13. SoW-Specific Terms** | Any terms that override or supplement the MSA for this engagement. |

### 4.3 Layaa AI-Specific SoW Provisions

- **Complexity Classification:** State the complexity level (Standard / Moderate / Complex / Highly Complex / Experimental) as it affects pricing.
- **Tool Disclosure:** List all third-party tools and APIs to be used (e.g., Claude API, OpenAI API, specific SaaS tools).
- **AI Output Disclaimer:** "Deliverables may incorporate outputs generated with the assistance of AI tools. Layaa AI applies human review and quality assurance to all AI-assisted outputs but does not guarantee that AI-generated content is free of all errors."

## 5. Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

### 5.1 Purpose

Standalone NDA used when confidential information is exchanged before an MSA is signed, or with parties who are not clients (partners, vendors, potential investors).

### 5.2 Structure

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **1. Parties** | Layaa AI Private Limited and the counterparty. Mutual NDA preferred. |
| **2. Definition of Confidential Information** | Broad definition covering business, technical, financial, and strategic information. Includes written, oral, visual, and electronic forms. |
| **3. Exclusions** | Publicly available information. Independently developed. Received from third party without restriction. Required by law (with notice to discloser). |
| **4. Obligations** | Reasonable care (at least same as own confidential information). Use only for the stated purpose. Limit access to need-to-know personnel. |
| **5. Term** | Agreement term: 2 years from effective date (or until MSA supersedes). Confidentiality obligation survives for 3 years post-termination (trade secrets: indefinite). |
| **6. Return / Destruction** | Upon request or termination, return or destroy all confidential materials. Certify destruction in writing. Exceptions for legally required archival copies. |
| **7. Remedies** | Acknowledge that breach may cause irreparable harm. Injunctive relief available without proving actual damages. |
| **8. General** | No licence or IP transfer. No obligation to enter into further agreement. Governing law (India). Jurisdiction (Gurgaon). |

### 5.3 NDA Variations

| Scenario | Variation |
|----------|-----------|
| Pre-sales discussion | Mutual NDA, 1-year term, general purpose |
| Vendor/tool evaluation | Mutual NDA, 2-year term, specific to evaluation |
| Partnership exploration | Mutual NDA, 2-year term, covers business and technical information |
| Contractor onboarding | One-way (contractor to Layaa AI), survives engagement + 3 years |

## 6. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

### 6.1 Purpose

Required whenever Layaa AI processes personal data on behalf of a client (acting as Data Processor under DPDP Act 2023).

### 6.2 Structure

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **1. Definitions** | Aligned with DPDP Act 2023: "Data Principal", "Data Fiduciary", "Data Processor", "Personal Data", "Processing", "Data Breach" |
| **2. Roles** | Client is Data Fiduciary. Layaa AI is Data Processor. Layaa AI processes data only on documented instructions from the Client. |
| **3. Scope of Processing** | Categories of data subjects. Types of personal data. Purpose of processing. Duration. |
| **4. Obligations of Layaa AI (Processor)** | Process only per written instructions. Implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures. Assist Client with data subject rights requests. Not engage sub-processors without prior written consent. |
| **5. Sub-Processors** | List of approved sub-processors (e.g., cloud hosting providers, API providers). Notification of new sub-processors. Right to object. Flow-down of obligations. |
| **6. Security Measures** | Encryption (in transit and at rest). Access controls. Regular security assessments. Incident response procedures. |
| **7. Data Breach Notification** | Notify Client within 24 hours of becoming aware of a breach. Provide details: nature, categories of data, approximate number of data subjects, likely consequences, remedial measures. Client responsible for 72-hour notification to Data Protection Board (if required under DPDP Act). |
| **8. Cross-Border Transfers** | Personal data not transferred outside India except to jurisdictions notified by the Central Government or with Client's explicit prior consent. Appropriate safeguards in place. |
| **9. Data Subject Rights** | Layaa AI assists Client in responding to requests from Data Principals (access, correction, erasure, grievance). |
| **10. Audit Rights** | Client may audit Layaa AI's data processing practices with reasonable notice (30 days). Layaa AI provides necessary information and access. Audit frequency: once per year (unless breach occurs). |
| **11. Data Retention & Deletion** | Upon termination or completion of processing purpose, delete or return all personal data. Certification of deletion. Exceptions for legal retention requirements. |
| **12. Liability** | Layaa AI liable for damage caused by non-compliance with DPA or processing outside Client instructions. Liability cap as per MSA. |

### 6.3 Layaa AI-Specific DPA Provisions

- **AI Processing Disclosure:** "Where personal data is processed using AI/ML models, Layaa AI shall disclose the nature of such processing and ensure that no personal data is used to train third-party AI models without explicit Client consent."
- **API Provider Sub-Processing:** "The Client acknowledges that Layaa AI utilises third-party AI API providers (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI) for certain processing tasks. Data sent to such APIs is subject to the respective provider's data processing terms, which are disclosed to the Client."
- **Minimal Data Principle:** "Layaa AI shall apply data minimisation principles, processing only the personal data strictly necessary for the stated purpose."

## 7. Freelancer / Contractor Agreement

### 7.1 Purpose

Used when Layaa AI engages individual freelancers or independent contractors for project work.

### 7.2 Structure

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **1. Parties** | Layaa AI Private Limited and the Contractor (individual or entity) |
| **2. Engagement** | Independent contractor relationship (not employment). No benefits, no exclusivity (unless separately agreed). |
| **3. Scope of Work** | Detailed description of services, deliverables, and standards. Reference to specific project/SoW. |
| **4. Term** | Start and end date. Extension mechanism. Early termination (15 days' notice by either party). |
| **5. Compensation** | Fee structure (fixed, hourly, milestone-based). Payment schedule. Invoice requirements. TDS deduction disclosure (Section 194J or 194C as applicable, currently 10% or 2%). |
| **6. Intellectual Property** | All work product created under this agreement is "work for hire" and belongs to Layaa AI (or Client, as directed). Contractor assigns all IP rights. Contractor warrants originality and non-infringement. |
| **7. Confidentiality** | Comprehensive NDA-equivalent clause. Covers Layaa AI information and client information. Survives termination by 3 years. |
| **8. Non-Solicitation** | Contractor shall not directly solicit Layaa AI's clients for 12 months after engagement ends. Note: broad non-compete is void under Section 27 of Indian Contract Act; restrict to non-solicitation only. |
| **9. Data Protection** | Contractor processes data only as instructed. Returns/deletes all data upon completion. No retention of personal data. |
| **10. Representations & Warranties** | Contractor has legal right to work. Work is original. No conflict with other obligations. Complies with applicable law. |
| **11. Indemnification** | Contractor indemnifies Layaa AI against IP infringement claims, confidentiality breaches, and tax-related claims (misclassification risk). |
| **12. Liability** | Limited to fees paid under the agreement. No consequential damages. |
| **13. Termination** | For convenience: 15 days' notice. For cause: immediate (material breach, confidentiality breach, quality failure after written warning). Payment for completed work upon termination. |
| **14. Independent Contractor Status** | Express declaration of independent contractor relationship. Contractor responsible for own taxes, insurance, equipment. No employment relationship created. No right to bind Layaa AI. |
| **15. General** | Governing law (India). Jurisdiction (Gurgaon). Arbitration per standard clause. Entire agreement. Amendment in writing. |

### 7.3 Layaa AI-Specific Contractor Provisions

- **Quality Gate:** "All deliverables are subject to review and approval by a Layaa AI Founder-Director before acceptance and payment."
- **Tool Usage:** "Contractor shall use Layaa AI-specified tools and platforms for collaboration, version control, and communication during the engagement."
- **Client Interaction:** "Contractor shall not interact directly with Layaa AI's clients unless expressly authorised in writing. All client communications go through Layaa AI."
- **TDS Clause:** "Layaa AI will deduct TDS at the applicable rate on all payments. Contractor is responsible for filing their own tax returns and claiming TDS credit."

## 8. Contract Execution Checklist

Before signing any contract, verify:

- [ ] Correct legal entity name (Layaa AI Private Limited) and CIN
- [ ] Registered office address is current
- [ ] Authorised signatory is a Director (Abhimanyu Singh or Shubham Sharma)

- [ ] Termination provisions are balanced
- [ ] Both parties' details (name, address, PAN/GSTIN) are correct
