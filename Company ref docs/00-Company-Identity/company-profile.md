## 8. Products & Platforms

### 8.1 Layaa OS — Multi-Agent AI Workforce Platform

Layaa OS is a self-hosted multi-agent AI workforce platform comprising 22 specialised AI agents. It powers all internal operations and external service delivery for Layaa AI.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Architecture** | PocketBase (backend) + React/TypeScript (frontend) + n8n (orchestration) + Google ADK (agent framework) + WebSockets (real-time) |
| **AI Models** | Claude Sonnet 4.6 (primary reasoning), Claude Haiku 4.5 (fast tasks) |
| **Agent Count** | 22 specialised agents across 10 functional areas |
| **Phase 0 (MVP)** | Complete — core agent routing, memory system, task execution |
| **Phase 1 (Real-Time Orchestration)** | In progress — live agent collaboration, WebSocket streaming, multi-agent handoff |

## 11. Competitive Edge

1. **AI-Native Operations:** Layaa AI runs on its own AI workforce (Layaa OS), meaning every process — from client delivery to internal strategy — is AI-augmented. This is not theory; it is production reality.

2. **SME-First Design:** All products, pricing, and delivery models are architected for the constraints and realities of Indian SMEs — limited tech teams, budget sensitivity, and the need for fast ROI.

3. **No-Code / Low-Code Delivery:** By building on n8n, Make, and similar platforms, Layaa AI delivers automation that clients can understand, maintain, and extend — reducing long-term vendor dependency.

4. **Speed to Value:** The 5-stage delivery methodology (Discovery to Enablement) is designed for 30-60 day ROI realisation, not multi-quarter transformation programmes.

5. **Full-Stack Capability:** From education and consulting through to build, support, and pre-built modules, Layaa AI covers the entire AI adoption lifecycle under one roof.

6. **Government-Recognised Startup:** DPIIT recognition (valid till 2035) and Udyam MSME registration provide credibility, tax benefits, and access to government procurement channels.

7. **Founder Complementarity:** CEO (strategy, BD, client relations) and CTO (technology, architecture, delivery) cover the full business-to-engineering spectrum without gaps.

8. **Self-Hosted & Data-Sovereign:** Infrastructure runs on Indian VPS with PocketBase and Backblaze B2, ensuring client data never leaves controlled environments — a critical trust factor for Indian businesses.
