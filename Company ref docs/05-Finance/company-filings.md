| **DPT-3** | Return of Deposits | By 30 June every year | INR 5,000 + INR 500/day of default |
| **DIR-8** | Intimation of Disqualification | At first Board Meeting of every FY | Penalty for non-filing |
| **MSME-1** | Return of outstanding payments to MSMEs | Half-yearly (by 31 October and 30 April) | INR 20,000 (up to INR 5,00,000 for continuing default) |
| **BEN-2** | Declaration of Significant Beneficial Ownership | Within 30 days of receipt of BEN-1 | INR 50,000 (up to INR 5,00,000) |

### 4.2 Detailed Filing Requirements

#### DIR-3 KYC (Director KYC)

- **Who:** Every director with an allotted DIN
- **When:** By 30 September of every financial year
- **What:** Verification of personal details, mobile, email, address
- **Form:** DIR-3 KYC (web-based if no changes; eForm if changes exist)
- **Penalty:** INR 5,000 per director; DIN marked as "Deactivated" until KYC is filed
- **Action for Layaa AI:** Both Abhimanyu Singh and Shubham Sharma must file individually

#### ADT-1 (Appointment of Auditor)

- **Who:** The company
- **When:** Within 15 days of the AGM at which the auditor is appointed
- **What:** Intimation to ROC of auditor appointment/re-appointment
- **First Auditor:** Appointed by Board within 30 days of incorporation (holds until first AGM)
- **Subsequent:** Appointed at AGM for a 5-year term (must be ratified annually if required by articles)
- **Penalty:** INR 300 per day of default (company); INR 100 per day (officers in default)

#### AOC-4 (Financial Statements)

- **Who:** The company
- **When:** Within 30 days of the AGM
- **What:** Filing of audited Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss Account, Cash Flow Statement, notes, auditor's report, Board's report
- **Attachment:** Director's Report, Auditor's Report, Financial Statements (XBRL for applicable companies; Layaa AI likely exempt as small company)
- **Penalty:** INR 100 per day for the company; officers in default also penalised

#### MGT-7 / MGT-7A (Annual Return)

- **Who:** The company
- **What:** MGT-7A (small company / OPC simplified form) -- Layaa AI likely qualifies as a small company
- **When:** Within 60 days of the AGM
- **Contents:** Registered office, principal business activities, shareholding pattern, directors, meetings held, remuneration of directors and KMPs, penalties/punishments
- **Penalty:** INR 100 per day of default (max INR 5,00,000 for the company)

#### DPT-3 (Return of Deposits)

- **Who:** Every company that has accepted deposits or has outstanding loans/receipts from members/public
- **When:** By 30 June every year
- **What:** Details of money received by the company that is not considered a deposit (exempted deposits) or actual deposits
- **Relevance:** Even if Layaa AI has not accepted deposits, if there are any outstanding loans from directors/members, DPT-3 must be filed
- **Penalty:** INR 5,000 initial + INR 500 per day of continuing default

#### DIR-8 (Intimation of Disqualification)

- **Who:** Every director
- **When:** At the first Board Meeting of every financial year
- **What:** Disclosure that the director is not disqualified under Section 164(2) of Companies Act 2013
- **Action:** Both directors submit Form DIR-8 at the first Board Meeting after 1 April each year

## 5. AGM (Annual General Meeting) Requirements

### 5.1 Timeline

| Requirement | Rule |
|------------|------|
| First AGM | Within 9 months from the close of the first financial year (i.e., by 31 December 2026 for FY ending March 2026) |
| Subsequent AGMs | Within 6 months from close of FY (i.e., by 30 September) |
| Gap between AGMs | Not more than 15 months between two consecutive AGMs |
| Extension | ROC can grant up to 3-month extension (not for first AGM) |

### 5.2 AGM Agenda (Standard)

1. Adoption of audited financial statements
2. Declaration of dividend (if any)
3. Appointment/ratification of auditor and fixing remuneration
4. Appointment of directors (if any retiring by rotation -- not applicable if only 2 directors and both are non-rotational as per Articles)
5. Any special business as per notice

### 5.3 Notice Requirements

- Minimum 21 clear days' notice (shorter notice allowed with consent of 95% of members for private company)
- Notice must include: place, date, time, agenda, explanatory statement for special business
- Serve on all members, directors, and auditor

### 5.4 Quorum

- For private company: 2 members personally present (if total members are 2, both must be present)

## 6. Board Meeting Requirements

### 6.1 Frequency

| Requirement | Rule |
|------------|------|
| Minimum per year | 4 Board Meetings per financial year |
| Maximum gap | Not more than 120 days between two consecutive meetings |
| First meeting | Within 30 days of incorporation |
| Quorum | One-third of total strength or 2 directors, whichever is higher (for Layaa AI: both directors) |

### 6.2 Suggested Board Meeting Schedule

| Quarter | Suggested Month | Key Agenda |
|---------|----------------|------------|
| Q1 (Apr--Jun) | April / May | DIR-8 disclosure, review FY results, approve annual accounts for audit, DPT-3 review |
| Q2 (Jul--Sep) | July / August | Pre-AGM preparation, approve Board Report, approve financial statements |
| Q3 (Oct--Dec) | October / November | Post-AGM actions, review half-year performance, compliance review |
| Q4 (Jan--Mar) | January / February | Review YTD performance, next FY planning, DIR-3 KYC reminder |

### 6.3 Board Meeting Notices

- Minimum 7 days' notice (can be shorter with consent of all directors)
- Written notice to every director at their registered address
- Agenda and notes on agenda items to be attached

### 6.4 Minutes

- Minutes must be prepared within 30 days of the meeting
- Maintained in a Minutes Book (physical or electronic)
- Signed by the Chairman of the meeting or the next meeting
- Preserved permanently
