## 1. Purpose

This document provides a month-by-month compliance calendar for Layaa AI Private Limited covering all statutory deadlines for the financial year (April to March). Every deadline includes the applicable penalty for non-compliance. This calendar must be reviewed at the start of each month.

---

## 2. Recurring Monthly Obligations

These apply **every month** of the financial year and are not repeated in the monthly breakdown below.

| Obligation | Due Date | Details | Penalty for Default |
|-----------|----------|---------|---------------------|
| **GSTR-1** (Outward Supply Return) | 11th of following month | Details of all outward supplies (sales invoices) for the month. Filed on GST portal. | INR 50/day CGST + INR 50/day SGST = INR 100/day (max INR 5,000 per return). Late fee charged in the next GSTR-3B filing. |
| **GSTR-3B** (Summary Return + Tax Payment) | 20th of following month | Summary of outward/inward supplies, ITC claimed, and net tax payable. Tax must be paid at the time of filing. | INR 50/day CGST + INR 50/day SGST = INR 100/day (max INR 5,000). Interest at 18% p.a. on late tax payment. |
| **TDS Deposit** | 7th of following month (except March: by 30 April) | Deposit TDS deducted during the previous month with the government via challan on TRACES/TIN-NSDL. | 1.5% per month (or part of month) on TDS amount from date of deduction to date of deposit. Interest is mandatory even if TDS return is filed on time. |
| **Invoice Compliance** | Continuous | Every invoice must carry GSTIN, SAC code (998314), HSN (if applicable), and correct GST rate (18%). E-invoicing mandatory if turnover exceeds INR 5 Crore in any FY from 2017-18 (not currently applicable to Layaa AI). | Penalty under Section 122 of CGST Act: INR 10,000 or tax amount, whichever is higher. |

## 5. Penalty Quick Reference

### 5.1 GST Penalties

| Default | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Late GSTR-1 | INR 50/day CGST + INR 50/day SGST (max INR 5,000 per return) |
| Late GSTR-3B | INR 50/day CGST + INR 50/day SGST (max INR 5,000) + 18% interest on late tax |
| Late GSTR-9 | INR 100/day CGST + INR 100/day SGST (max 0.50% of state turnover) |
| Non-issuance of invoice | INR 10,000 or tax amount, whichever is higher |
| Wrong ITC claim | 100% of wrong claim + 24% interest |

### 5.2 TDS Penalties

| Default | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Late deduction | 1% per month from due date of deduction to actual deduction date |
| Late deposit after deduction | 1.5% per month from deduction date to deposit date |
| Late TDS return filing | INR 200/day (max = total TDS amount in the return) |
| Non-issuance of TDS certificate | INR 100/day per certificate |
| Incorrect information in return | INR 10,000 to INR 1,00,000 |

### 5.3 Income Tax Penalties

| Default | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Late ITR filing | INR 5,000 (by 31 Dec) / INR 10,000 (after 31 Dec). For income below INR 5 Lakh: max INR 1,000 |
| Interest on late payment | Section 234A: 1% per month on outstanding tax |
| Short advance tax payment | Section 234B: 1% per month. Section 234C: 1% per month per quarter |
| Under-reporting of income | 50% of tax on under-reported income |
| Misreporting of income | 200% of tax on misreported income |

### 5.4 MCA Penalties

| Default | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Late AOC-4 | INR 100/day (no cap specified) |
| Late MGT-7/7A | INR 100/day (max INR 5,00,000) |
| Late ADT-1 | INR 300/day (max INR 12,00,000) |
| DIR-3 KYC default | INR 5,000 per director + DIN deactivation |
| Not holding AGM | INR 1,00,000 (company) + INR 25,000 per officer |
| Not holding Board Meeting | INR 25,000 per officer |
| Late DPT-3 | INR 5,000 + INR 500/day |
