# Layaa AI — Revenue Model & Pricing

## Revenue Structure

### Implementation Fee (One-Time)
- **Average:** ₹2.5 Lakhs (50% deposit on contract signing)
- **Range:** ₹50K (basic automation) to ₹10L+ (complex multi-system integration)
- Covers: Discovery, assessment, development, validation, enablement

### Monthly Retainer Tiers
| Tier | Monthly Fee | Includes |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Starter** | ₹15,000/month | Basic monitoring, minor updates, email support |
| **Growth** | ₹40,000/month | Active monitoring, monthly optimizations, priority support, performance reviews |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Dedicated support, continuous development, SLA-backed |

### Pricing Philosophy (from Veer's Engine v2)
1. Never compete on price alone — compete on value, speed, and methodology
2. Minimum viable budget: ₹50K+ implementation (below this, deals are unprofitable)
3. Margin targets by vertical: Education 50-60%, Consulting 40-50%, Development 35-45%, Maintenance 60-70%, Pre-built 70%+
4. Discounts >20% require Founder approval
5. All pricing includes 18% GST (charged separately on invoice)
6. Payment terms: 50% advance, 50% on delivery (standard); custom terms need Founder approval

### Hybrid Pricing Formula
```
Total Price = Fixed Component + Variable Component + Value-Based Tier
Where:
  Fixed = Base hours × hourly rate × complexity multiplier
  Variable = API costs + tool subscriptions + integration complexity
  Value-Based = Revenue impact premium (for high-ROI automations)
```

## Red Flag Thresholds (Escalate to Founders)
- Margin below 30% on any deal
- CAC:LTV ratio below 2.5x
- Discount request >20%
- Customer concentration >30% of revenue
- Payment terms >60 days
