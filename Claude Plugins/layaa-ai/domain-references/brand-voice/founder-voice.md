# Layaa AI — Founder Voice Profiles

## Shared Traits (Both Founders)
- Conversational and accessible, never corporate
- Opinionated but diplomatic — take positions thoughtfully
- Balance personal "I" with company "we" based on context
- Vary sentence length intentionally for rhythm
- Avoid: profanity, jargon, unvalidated claims, polarizing political commentary
- Preferred terms: "teams" > "organizations", "building" > "developing", "learning" > "discovering", "clarity" > "alignment", "execution" > "implementation"

## Abhimanyu Singh — Co-Founder (Strategy & Business Development)

### Signature Expressions
- "Here's what we're learning..."
- "The real challenge is..."
- "In building Layaa AI, we've seen..."
- "Most people focus on X, but Y matters more"
- "It's not about X, it's about Y" (reframing assumptions)

### Topics He Gravitates To
- AI ethics and responsible deployment
- Future of work and human-AI collaboration
- Business strategy and operational excellence
- Building in public — sharing the Layaa AI journey authentically

### Angles He Prefers
- Practical insights over theory
- Experience-based perspectives
- Balanced views that acknowledge complexity
- Human-centered AI narratives (AI as tool, not replacement)

### Editing Patterns
- Adds personal context from Layaa AI's journey
- Softens absolutes ("always" → "often", "never" → "rarely")
- Removes anything salesy or promotional
- Ensures claims are backed by experience
- Flags missing "why it matters" context

### Approved Voice Examples
> "We're building Layaa AI to solve a problem we kept seeing: teams drowning in tasks that AI should handle. Not the exciting work. The repetitive stuff that kills momentum. Here's what we've learned about making AI actually useful..."

> "Three months into building our AI workforce platform. The hardest part isn't the technology. It's figuring out which workflows actually matter to automate."

> "Most AI tools promise transformation. We're focused on something simpler: giving your team back time to think."

## Shubham Sharma — Co-Founder (Technology & Delivery)

### Signature Expressions
- "Here's what we're seeing..."
- "The question we're asking is..."
- "Building Layaa AI has taught us..."
- "The real work is in..." (highlighting what's often overlooked)
- "What's becoming clear is..." (emerging insight)

### Topics He Gravitates To
- Technical architecture and automation patterns
- How AI actually works in practice (demystifying)
- Building reliable systems for SMEs
- The gap between AI promise and AI reality

### Angles He Prefers
- Technical depth made accessible
- Problem-solving narratives
- Behind-the-scenes of building automation systems
- What works now vs. speculative predictions

### Editing Patterns
- Similar to Abhimanyu (shared founding sensibility)
- Adds technical credibility without jargon
- Ensures balanced view of capabilities and limitations
- Removes overpromising language

### Approved Voice Examples
> "We're seeing teams struggle with a gap between AI promise and AI reality. The tools exist. The workflows don't. At Layaa AI, we're focused on closing that gap."

> "Building an AI company means constantly questioning what actually needs to be automated. Not everything should be. Here's how we decide what matters..."

> "AI can do a lot. But what should it do for your team? That's the question we're answering at Layaa AI."

## Cross-Founder Rules (Non-Negotiable)
- No fabricated statistics or fake testimonials
- No overpromising on AI capabilities
- No jargon-heavy corporate speak
- No hype-driven language without substance
- Evidence-based claims only (validated or clearly framed as hypothesis)
- Founder approval required for messaging direction shifts
