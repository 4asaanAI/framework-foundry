# Layaa AI — Channel Playbooks

## Channel Strategy Philosophy
- Efficiency over scale — low CAC is critical for SMB economics
- Intent over interruption — target prospects actively seeking solutions
- Education over promotion — build trust through value, not hard selling
- Compounding returns — favor channels with long-term asset building

## LinkedIn Playbook

### Organic Strategy
- **Content mix:** 40% thought leadership, 30% educational, 20% building in public, 10% product updates
- **Founders (target):** 3-5 posts/week each
- **Founders (current operating cadence):** 1 post/week on personal LinkedIn — confirmed 2026-03-29. Weekly cadence chosen to avoid exhausting unique content too quickly. Stale threshold = 7 days (not 3).
- **Company page:** 2-3 posts/week (target)
- **Engagement:** Comment on target communities, engage with SMB founders
- **Success metrics:** Engagement rate 3-8%, profile views from ICP, inbound demo requests
- **Escalation:** Engagement below 2% for 2+ weeks → review content strategy

### Paid Advertising
- **Thought Leadership Amplification (20-30%):** Boost top organic content, broad ICP, CPC target $5-10
- **Lead Generation (50-60%):** Demo requests + lead magnets, narrow ICP, CPC target $8-15
- **Retargeting (10-20%):** Website visitors + engaged audience, conversion focus

## Google Search Playbook
- **Focus:** High-intent keywords (AI automation for SMEs, workflow automation India, etc.)
- **Strategy:** Bottom-of-funnel first (demo/pricing searches), then expand to mid-funnel
- **Landing pages:** Dedicated per service vertical
- **Budget:** Start small, scale on proven ROI

## Content Marketing + SEO
- **Content pillars:** AI for SMEs, automation case studies, workflow optimization, future of work
- **Format:** Blog posts (1000-2500 words), whitepapers, how-to guides
- **Frequency:** 2-3 blog posts/week
- **SEO targets:** "AI automation for small business India", "workflow automation", "SME AI solutions"

## Email Marketing
- **Sequences:** Welcome → Value → Case Study → Offer → Follow-up
- **Per email:** 100-200 words, 6-8 word subject line
- **Segmentation:** By ICP, by funnel stage, by engagement level
- **Metrics:** Open rate >25%, CTR >3%, unsubscribe <0.5%

## Referral & Community
- **Referral program:** Client referrals with incentives (month free, feature unlocks)
- **Community:** LinkedIn groups, founder communities, industry associations
- **Partnerships:** Complementary tech vendors, consultancies, industry bodies
