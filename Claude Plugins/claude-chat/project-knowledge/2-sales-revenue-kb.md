## Battle Cards by Persona

**Overwhelmed SME Founder (10-50 employees)**
- Pain: Drowning in ops, can't hire for every role, growth limited by personal capacity
- Message: "Get your time back to focus on growing your business, not managing operations."
- Supporting: Built for SMEs (not enterprise pricing), we operate it (no IT burden), fast implementation
- Objections: "Too expensive" → compare to one hire (₹4-8L/year + overhead) | "No time" → we handle everything | "Not sure it works" → POC approach

**Operations Manager (20-100 employees)**
- Message: "Scale your operations without scaling complexity or headcount."
- Objections: "Need to see it first" → pilot approach | "Integration concerns" → custom-built for your tools

**Finance/Admin Head (15-75 employees)**
- Message: "Automate repetitive admin so you can focus on strategic finance operations."
- Best fit: Invoice/expense/report automation

**Tech-Savvy Business Owner**
- Message: "We build what you'd build yourself, and operate it for you."

| "Comparing with competitor" | Custom vs. template. We operate vs. you maintain. SME pricing. | → Kabir for intel |

## Sales Process
1. Prospect identified → research ICP profile and fit
2. Initial outreach → persona-specific messaging from battle cards
3. Discovery call → Rohit's 5-phase framework
4. Proposal → Veer's pricing engine + standard templates
5. Negotiation → objection handling playbook; pricing always to Veer

7. Onboard → Discovery → Architecture → Build → Validate → Enable

## Revenue Operations

### Pipeline Benchmarks
- MQL → SQL: 25% | SQL → Proposal: 60% | Proposal → Won: 35% | End-to-end: ~5.25%

### Pipeline Health Signals
- 🟢 Healthy: Regular contact, clear next steps, close date defined, budget confirmed
- 🟡 Watch: No contact >7 days, unclear next step, budget not confirmed, stakeholder changed
- 🔴 At Risk: No contact >14 days, deal on hold, budget cut, competitor shortlisted

### Deal Velocity (Expected Days)
- MQL → First contact: 1-2 days | First contact → Discovery: 7-14 days
- Discovery → Proposal: 5-7 days | Proposal → Decision: 14-30 days
- Total average cycle: 45-75 days

### Forecast Methodology
- Conservative: pipeline × close rates × 0.9
- Base: pipeline × close rates
- Optimistic: pipeline × close rates × 1.1 + late-stage upsides
- Weighted by stage: Proposal 35% | SQL 21% | MQL 5.25%
