## Legal Framework

### Standard Contract Terms

### Red Flag Clauses (Never accept without Founder review)
- Unlimited liability
- IP vesting in client before full payment received
- Exclusivity clauses (limits Layaa AI from serving other clients)
- Unilateral amendment rights (client can change terms unilaterally)
- Audit rights without reasonable notice (less than 5 business days)
- Non-compete broader than 12 months or very broad scope
- Jurisdiction outside India
- Indemnification for third-party IP claims beyond Layaa AI's control

### NDA Classification Framework
🟢 **GREEN — Sign as-is:** Mutual confidentiality | Reasonable scope | Term 1-3 years | Standard carve-outs | Layaa AI retains right to use anonymised learnings

🟡 **YELLOW — Negotiate before signing:** One-sided but not extreme | Broad but not unlimited scope | Term >3 years | Missing standard carve-outs → Specify changes needed

🔴 **RED — Founder/legal review required:** Unlimited scope | IP grab | Long non-compete | Jurisdiction outside India | Perpetual term with no sunset

### Key Standard Clauses

**Scope of Work:**
"Services shall be limited to those described in Schedule A. Any additional scope requires a written change order signed by both parties, accompanied by revised pricing."

**Payment:**
"Client shall pay 50% of total fees upon execution ('Advance Payment') and 50% upon delivery ('Balance Payment'). All amounts are exclusive of applicable taxes including GST at 18%."

**IP:**
"Upon receipt of full payment, all work product created specifically for Client shall vest in Client. Layaa AI retains ownership of its pre-existing IP, tools, frameworks, and methodology. Layaa AI retains the right to use anonymised learnings for internal improvement."

**Liability:**
"Layaa AI's total liability shall not exceed total fees paid by Client in the three (3) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages."

**Confidentiality:**
"Each party shall maintain confidentiality of the other's Confidential Information for two (2) years following termination. This does not apply to publicly available information, independently developed information, or information required to be disclosed by law."

## Indian Regulatory Context

### DPDP Act 2023 (Digital Personal Data Protection)
- Consent required before processing personal data
- Data principal rights: access, correction, erasure, grievance redressal
- Data fiduciary obligations: purpose limitation, storage limitation, security
- Cross-border transfer: only to notified countries
- **Impact on Layaa AI:** Client data in workflows must have consent basis; cannot repurpose client data; build audit trail into every workflow handling personal data

### IT Act 2000
- Electronic contracts are valid and enforceable in India
- Intermediary liability protection if Layaa AI acts as intermediary
- Cybersecurity obligations for IT companies

### GST Act
- 18% GST on all professional services
- CGST 9% + SGST 9% for same-state | IGST 18% for inter-state
- Invoice requirements: GST number, SAC code, place of supply
- TDS on professional services: typically 10% deducted by client (not Layaa AI's liability)

### MSME Act (Payment Protection)
- Layaa AI is UDYAM registered MSME
- Clients must pay MSME suppliers within 45 days
- Late payment: compound interest at 3× bank rate

### Key Financial Metrics
| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Gross Margin | (Revenue - Direct Costs) / Revenue | >35% (varies by vertical) |
| CAC | Total Sales & Marketing Spend / New Clients | Track monthly |
| LTV | Average Monthly Revenue × Average Client Lifespan (months) | CAC:LTV ≥ 2.5x |
| MRR Growth | (This MRR - Last MRR) / Last MRR | Track monthly |
| DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) | (AR / Revenue) × 30 | <45 days |
| Cash Runway | Cash / Monthly Burn Rate | >6 months |

### Red Flag Thresholds (Escalate to Founders)
- Gross margin below 30% on any engagement
- CAC:LTV ratio below 2.5x
- Discount request >20%
- Client revenue concentration >30% of total revenue
- Payment terms requested >60 days
- Accounts receivable aging >60 days for any single client
- Cash runway below 3 months
