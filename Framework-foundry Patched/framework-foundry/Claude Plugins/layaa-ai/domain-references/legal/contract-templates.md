# Layaa AI — Contract Templates

## Template Index

| Template | When to Use | Binding? |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Mutual NDA | Before any confidential discussions | Yes |
| Master Services Agreement (MSA) | Every paid engagement | Yes |
| Statement of Work (SoW) | Each project under an MSA | Yes |
| Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) | Partnerships, strategic relationships | Partially (only confidentiality) |

## 1. Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

**Use when:** Before sharing confidential information with prospects, partners, or vendors.

### Structure

```
MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

Date: [DD/MM/YYYY]

BETWEEN:
(1) Layaa AI Private Limited, CIN: U62099HR2025PTC139528,
    registered at [Registered Address], Gurgaon, Haryana ("Party A")
(2) [Company Name], [Registration Number],
    registered at [Address] ("Party B")

RECITALS:
The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship and may
need to share confidential information.

1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
   All non-public information disclosed by either party, whether oral,
   written, electronic, or visual, including but not limited to:
   business plans, pricing, customer data, technical specifications,
   source code, methodologies, and financial information.

2. OBLIGATIONS
   The receiving party shall: (a) maintain confidentiality using at
   least the same care as its own confidential information; (b) use
   confidential information solely for the Purpose; (c) limit access
   to authorized personnel with need-to-know.

3. EXCLUSIONS
   Information that: (a) is publicly available; (b) was known prior
   to disclosure; (c) is independently developed; (d) is received
   from a third party without restriction; (e) is required by law
   (with prompt notice to disclosing party).

4. TERM
   This Agreement is effective for two (2) years from the date above.
   Confidentiality obligations survive for three (3) years from
   disclosure.

5. RETURN OF INFORMATION
   Upon request or termination, receiving party shall return or
   destroy all confidential information within fifteen (15) days.

6. REMEDIES
   Breach may cause irreparable harm; equitable relief available
   without posting bond.

7. GOVERNING LAW
   Laws of India. Courts of Gurgaon, Haryana have exclusive
   jurisdiction.

SIGNATURES:
[Party A]                    [Party B]
Name:                        Name:
Title:                       Title:
Date:                        Date:
```

**Customize:** Party B details, term length if non-standard, specific categories of confidential information if needed.

## 3. Statement of Work (SoW)

**Use when:** Each project under an MSA. References the MSA for general terms.

### Structure

```
STATEMENT OF WORK

SoW No: LAI-SOW-[YYYY]-[XXX]
Under MSA dated: [DD/MM/YYYY], Agreement No: LAI-MSA-[YYYY]-[XXX]

1. PROJECT NAME
   [Descriptive project name]

2. BACKGROUND
   [Brief context — client's business, problem being solved]

3. SCOPE OF SERVICES
   Layaa AI shall deliver the following:
   3.1 [Deliverable 1 — detailed description]
   3.2 [Deliverable 2 — detailed description]
   3.3 [Deliverable 3 — detailed description]

   OUT OF SCOPE:
   - [Explicitly list what is NOT included]

4. TIMELINE & MILESTONES
   | Milestone | Description | Target Date |
   |-----------|-------------|-------------|
   | Kickoff | Project initiation, access setup | Week 1 |
   | Discovery | Requirements gathering, process mapping | Week 2-3 |
   | Development | Build and configure automations | Week 4-6 |
   | Testing | UAT, bug fixes, validation | Week 7 |
   | Go-Live | Deployment, enablement training | Week 8 |

5. PRICING
   5.1 Implementation Fee: ₹[Amount]
   5.2 Monthly Retainer: ₹[Amount]/month ([Starter/Growth/Enterprise])
   5.3 Retainer includes: [X] workflow runs, [Y] support hours, [Z] updates

6. PAYMENT SCHEDULE
   - 50% of Implementation Fee upon SoW execution: ₹[Amount]
   - 25% upon completion of Development milestone: ₹[Amount]
   - 25% upon Go-Live: ₹[Amount]
   - Monthly retainer begins on Go-Live date

7. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
   Deliverables are accepted when they materially conform to the
   specifications above. Client has ten (10) business days to review
   and accept or provide written objections.

8. CHANGE ORDER PROCESS
   Any changes to scope require a written Change Order signed by both
   parties, specifying: changed deliverables, timeline impact, cost
   impact.

9. PROJECT TEAM
   Service Provider:
   - Project Lead: [Name]
   - Technical Architect: [Name]
   Client:
   - Primary Contact: [Name, Email, Phone]
   - Technical Contact: [Name, Email, Phone]

SIGNATURES:
Service Provider              Client
Name:                          Name:
Title:                         Title:
Date:                          Date:
```

**Customize:** All bracketed fields. Scope section is critical — be specific to avoid scope creep. Always include OUT OF SCOPE section.

## 4. Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)

**Use when:** Strategic partnerships, referral arrangements, co-development discussions, or joint ventures that are exploratory.

### Structure

```
MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

Date: [DD/MM/YYYY]

BETWEEN:
(1) Layaa AI Private Limited ("Party A")
(2) [Partner Company] ("Party B")

1. PURPOSE
   This MoU outlines the mutual intention of the parties to explore
   [describe collaboration — e.g., joint go-to-market, referral
   partnership, technology integration].

2. SCOPE OF COLLABORATION
   2.1 [Party A's contributions/responsibilities]
   2.2 [Party B's contributions/responsibilities]
   2.3 [Joint activities]

3. TERM
   This MoU is effective for [6/12] months from the date above,
   renewable by mutual written agreement.

4. FINANCIAL ARRANGEMENTS
   [If applicable: referral fees, revenue sharing, cost sharing]
   [If not applicable: "No financial obligations arise from this MoU."]

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of information
   shared under this MoU. [Reference NDA if one exists, or insert
   confidentiality clause.]

6. NON-BINDING
   This MoU is a statement of intent and does not create legally
   binding obligations, EXCEPT for Section 5 (Confidentiality),
   which is binding.

7. TERMINATION
   Either party may terminate this MoU with fifteen (15) days'
   written notice.

8. GOVERNING LAW
   Laws of India.

SIGNATURES:
Party A                       Party B
Name:                          Name:
Title:                         Title:
Date:                          Date:
```

**Customize:** Purpose and scope are fully custom. Financial arrangements only if applicable. Always keep confidentiality binding even though MoU is non-binding.

## Template Selection Guide

| Situation | Template(s) Needed |
|-----------|-------------------|
| New client, first meeting | NDA |
| Client ready to engage | MSA + SoW |
| Additional project for existing client | SoW only (under existing MSA) |
| Referral partnership discussion | NDA + MoU |
| Technology integration partner | NDA + MoU → later MSA if commercial |
| Internal vendor engagement | NDA + MSA (Layaa AI as client) |
