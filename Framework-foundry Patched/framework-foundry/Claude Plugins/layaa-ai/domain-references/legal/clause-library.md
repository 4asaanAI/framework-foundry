# Layaa AI — Legal Clause Library

## How to Use This Library

Each clause below is a standard Layaa AI contract clause. Use as-is or customize per engagement. Clauses marked **NON-NEGOTIABLE** cannot be modified without Founder approval.

---

## 1. Scope of Services

**Usage:** Every client contract (MSA or SoW)

**Standard Text:**
> Layaa AI shall provide the Services as described in the applicable Statement of Work ("SoW"), which shall be executed by both parties and incorporated herein by reference. Any changes to the scope of Services shall be made through a written Change Order signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Specific deliverable descriptions, timelines, milestones
- Non-negotiable: Requirement for written Change Orders (protects against scope creep)
- Escalation: If client insists on open-ended scope → escalate to Founders

---

## 2. Intellectual Property

**Usage:** Every contract — **NON-NEGOTIABLE core terms**

**Standard Text:**
> **Pre-existing IP:** Each party retains all rights in its pre-existing intellectual property. Layaa AI retains ownership of all proprietary tools, frameworks, methodologies, libraries, and reusable components developed prior to or independent of this engagement ("Layaa AI IP").
>
> **Custom Deliverables:** Upon full payment, Client shall own the custom deliverables specifically created for Client as described in the SoW ("Custom Deliverables"). Custom Deliverables do not include Layaa AI IP.
>
> **License Grant:** Layaa AI grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to use any Layaa AI IP embedded in or necessary for the operation of the Custom Deliverables, solely for Client's internal business purposes.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Scope of license (can expand to affiliates if needed)
- Non-negotiable: Layaa AI retains pre-existing IP; work-for-hire language rejected
- Escalation: If client demands full IP transfer including frameworks → immediate Founder escalation

---

## 3. Confidentiality

**Usage:** Every engagement (standalone NDA or contract clause)

**Standard Text:**
> Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of Confidential Information received from the other party for a period of three (3) years from the date of disclosure. Confidential Information excludes information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available without breach; (b) was known to the receiving party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed without use of Confidential Information; (d) is disclosed pursuant to legal compulsion, provided the receiving party gives prompt notice.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Survival period (3 years standard, can extend to 5 for sensitive engagements)
- Non-negotiable: Mutual obligation (not one-sided); carve-outs must remain
- Escalation: If client wants perpetual confidentiality with no carve-outs → flag to Abhay

---

## 4. Payment Terms

**Usage:** Every contract — **NON-NEGOTIABLE core structure**

**Standard Text:**
> Client shall pay 50% of the implementation fee upon execution of this Agreement ("Deposit"). The remaining 50% shall be due upon delivery of the final deliverables. Monthly retainer fees are billed in advance on the 1st of each month, payable within fifteen (15) days of invoice date ("Net-15"). Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Layaa AI reserves the right to suspend Services if payment is overdue by more than thirty (30) days.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Payment milestones for large projects (can do 50/25/25 instead of 50/50)
- Non-negotiable: 50% upfront deposit; right to suspend for non-payment
- Escalation: If client requests net-60+ or <30% upfront → escalate to Founders

---

## 5. Limitation of Liability

**Usage:** Every contract — **NON-NEGOTIABLE**

**Standard Text:**
> The aggregate liability of either party under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid or payable by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages, including loss of profits, data, or business opportunity. These limitations do not apply to: (a) gross negligence or willful misconduct; (b) breach of confidentiality obligations; (c) IP infringement claims; (d) indemnification obligations.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Cap period (12 months is standard; can negotiate to 6 months for short projects)
- Non-negotiable: Must have a liability cap; must exclude indirect damages
- Escalation: If client demands unlimited liability → reject; if they want cap >2x fees → Founder approval

---

## 6. Indemnification

**Usage:** Every contract

**Standard Text:**
> **Layaa AI indemnifies Client** against third-party claims arising from: (a) infringement of intellectual property rights by Layaa AI's pre-existing IP; (b) gross negligence or willful misconduct by Layaa AI.
>
> **Client indemnifies Layaa AI** against third-party claims arising from: (a) Client's data, content, or materials provided to Layaa AI; (b) Client's use of deliverables in violation of applicable law.
>
> Indemnification obligations are subject to the limitation of liability cap set forth herein.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Specific carve-outs can be added for regulated industries
- Non-negotiable: Must be mutual; indemnification cap = liability cap
- Escalation: If client wants uncapped indemnification or one-sided → Founder escalation

---

## 7. Termination

**Usage:** Every contract

**Standard Text:**
> Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days' prior written notice. Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach by the other party, provided the breaching party fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days of written notice. Upon termination: (a) Client shall pay for all Services performed through the termination date; (b) Layaa AI shall deliver all work-in-progress; (c) the following provisions survive termination: Intellectual Property, Confidentiality, Limitation of Liability, and Dispute Resolution.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Notice period (can extend to 60 days for large engagements)
- Non-negotiable: Cure period for breach; payment for work completed; survival clauses
- Escalation: If client wants immediate termination without cure period → Abhay reviews

---

## 8. Data Protection

**Usage:** Every contract involving data processing

**Standard Text:**
> Layaa AI shall process personal data solely as instructed by Client and in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP Act"). Layaa AI shall implement reasonable security measures to protect personal data. In the event of a data breach, Layaa AI shall notify Client within seventy-two (72) hours. Upon termination, Layaa AI shall return or delete all personal data as directed by Client.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Specific security measures can be detailed in SoW
- Non-negotiable: DPDP Act compliance; 72-hour breach notification
- Escalation: If client requires data localization or specific certifications → Abhay + Preeti review

---

## 9. Force Majeure

**Usage:** Every contract

**Standard Text:**
> Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, government actions, pandemics, war, or infrastructure failures. The affected party shall notify the other within seven (7) days. If the force majeure event continues for more than ninety (90) days, either party may terminate this Agreement.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: List of qualifying events; notification period
- Non-negotiable: Must be mutual; must include termination right for extended events

---

## 10. Dispute Resolution

**Usage:** Every contract — **NON-NEGOTIABLE jurisdiction**

**Standard Text:**
> This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Any dispute shall be resolved by binding arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, with the seat of arbitration in Gurgaon, Haryana. Disputes involving amounts less than ₹50,00,000 shall be referred to a sole arbitrator; larger disputes to a panel of three arbitrators.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Mediation step before arbitration (can add if client prefers)
- Non-negotiable: Indian governing law; Gurgaon seat
- Escalation: If client demands foreign jurisdiction or foreign arbitration → reject or Founder approval

---

## 11. Warranty

**Usage:** Every contract with deliverables

**Standard Text:**
> Layaa AI warrants that Services shall be performed in a professional, workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards. Deliverables shall materially conform to the specifications in the SoW for a period of sixty (60) days following delivery ("Warranty Period"). Client's sole remedy for breach of warranty is re-performance at no additional cost. Layaa AI makes no warranty regarding third-party tools, APIs, or platforms.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Warranty period (60 days standard; can extend to 90 for enterprise)
- Non-negotiable: Remedy limited to re-performance; no warranty on third-party tools
- Escalation: If client wants open-ended warranty or money-back guarantee → Founder approval

---

## 12. Non-Solicitation

**Usage:** Recommended for all contracts

**Standard Text:**
> During the term of this Agreement and for twelve (12) months thereafter, neither party shall directly solicit for employment any employee of the other party who was involved in the performance of this Agreement. This restriction does not apply to general public job postings.

**Negotiation Guidance:**
- Flexible: Duration (12 months standard; can reduce to 6 if client pushes)
- Non-negotiable: Must be mutual
- Escalation: If client refuses non-solicitation entirely → flag to Abhay (low risk, can proceed)
