# Layaa AI — Compliance Calendar

## Annual Filing Schedule

### MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) Filings

| Filing | Form | Deadline | Escalation | Notes |
|--------|------|----------|------------|-------|
| Financial Statements | AOC-4 | Within 30 days of AGM | E1 (Founder) | Requires audited financials |
| Annual Return | MGT-7 / MGT-7A | Within 60 days of AGM | E1 (Founder) | MGT-7A for small companies |
| Auditor Appointment | ADT-1 | Within 15 days of AGM | E1 (Founder) | First auditor at first AGM |
| Director KYC | DIR-3 KYC | September 30 annually | E4 (Independent) | Each director files separately |
| Director Changes | DIR-12 | Within 30 days of change | E3 (Legal review) | If directors added/removed |
| Board Resolutions | MGT-14 | Within 30 days of resolution | E1 (Founder) | Only for specified resolutions |
| Commencement of Business | INC-20A | Within 180 days of incorporation | E1 (Founder) | One-time filing |
| MSME Return | MSME-1 | Oct 31, Apr 30 (half-yearly) | E1 (Founder) | Only if outstanding MSME payments |

### Board & AGM Requirements

| Requirement | Frequency | Rule |
|-------------|-----------|------|
| Board Meetings | Minimum 4 per year | Gap between consecutive meetings ≤120 days |
| AGM | Annual | Before September 30; first AGM within 9 months of incorporation |
| Minutes Recording | Every meeting | Within 30 days; signed by chairperson |

### GST Filings

| Filing | Form | Deadline | Notes |
|--------|------|----------|-------|
| Outward Supply Return | GSTR-1 | 11th of following month | Invoice-level details |
| Summary Return | GSTR-3B | 20th of following month | Tax payment |
| Annual Return | GSTR-9 | December 31 | For FY ending March 31 |

### Income Tax

| Filing | Deadline | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| ITR Filing | October 31 (if audit required) / July 31 | Company return |
| Tax Audit Report | September 30 | If turnover >₹1 crore |
| Advance Tax - Q1 | June 15 | 15% of estimated tax |
| Advance Tax - Q2 | September 15 | 45% cumulative |
| Advance Tax - Q3 | December 15 | 75% cumulative |
| Advance Tax - Q4 | March 15 | 100% cumulative |

### TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)

| Filing | Deadline | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| TDS Deposit | 7th of following month | Government challan |
| TDS Return (Salary) | Form 24Q - quarterly | Q1: July 31, Q2: Oct 31, Q3: Jan 31, Q4: May 31 |
| TDS Return (Non-salary) | Form 26Q - quarterly | Same dates as 24Q |
| TDS Certificate | Form 16/16A | Within 15 days of TDS return filing |

### Startup-Specific

| Filing | Deadline | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| DPIIT Annual Compliance | Annual self-certification | Maintain recognition status |
| Udyam Update | As needed | Update if turnover/investment changes |
| 80-IAC Application | Apply within first 10 years | 3-year tax holiday on approval |

## Alert Schedule

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| 60 days before deadline | First reminder — begin preparation |
| 30 days before deadline | Second reminder — draft completion target |
| 15 days before deadline | Escalation to Founders if not ready |
| 7 days before deadline | URGENT escalation — must file |

## Escalation Levels

| Level | Description | Process |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| E1 - Founder Approval | Standard filings requiring signature | Anne drafts → Founders approve → Submit |
| E2 - Strategy Review | Filings with strategic implications | Anne drafts → Kabir reviews → Founders approve |
| E3 - Legal Review | Filings with legal implications | Anne drafts → Abhay reviews → Founders approve |
| E4 - Independent | Director-specific filings | Individual director files independently |

## Penalty Reference

| Filing | Penalty for Late Filing |
|--------|------------------------|
| AOC-4 | ₹100/day (up to ₹5L for company + ₹1L for directors) |
| MGT-7 | ₹100/day (up to ₹5L for company + ₹1L for directors) |
| DIR-3 KYC | ₹5,000 fee + DIN deactivation |
| INC-20A | Company may be struck off after 180 days |
| GSTR-1/3B | ₹50/day (₹20/day for nil return) up to ₹10,000 |
| ITR | ₹5,000 (₹1,000 if income <₹5L); interest on tax due |
| TDS | 1.5% per month interest + ₹200/day penalty |
