# GTM-Revenue Alignment

Core principle: Leads are vanity metrics. Revenue is the truth.

---

## 4-Layer Alignment Model

```
Layer 1: Strategic Positioning
   |
Layer 2: Campaign Execution
   |
Layer 3: Sales Conversion
   |
Layer 4: Revenue Realization
```

RevOps monitors alignment across all 4 layers and surfaces disconnects.

---

## Revenue Attribution Rules

- **Model:** First-touch attribution with deal source validation.
- **First-touch:** Credit the campaign that generated the initial MQL.
- **Validation:** If deal closes 6+ months later, verify campaign still influenced decision (ask in closing call).
- **Attribution window:** 180 days (6 months) from MQL creation.
- **Exception:** Direct referrals attributed to "Referral Program" regardless of prior campaign touches.

## Campaign ROI Thresholds

| Status | ROI | CAC | Action |
|--------|-----|-----|--------|
| Green | >5x | <Rs 20k | Scale budget, replicate success factors |
| Yellow | 2-5x | Rs 20-40k | Optimize targeting, A/B test messaging |
| Red | <2x | >Rs 40k | Pause campaign, conduct root cause analysis |

ROI formula: `Revenue Generated / Campaign Spend`
CAC formula: `Campaign Spend / Deals Won`

## ICP Targeting Overlap Analysis

Compare marketing budget allocation against actual revenue contribution per ICP segment.

| ICP | Marketing Focus (% of Budget) | Revenue Contribution (% of Total) | Alignment Score |
|-----|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------|-----------------|
| [Segment] | X% | Y% | Aligned / Misaligned |

Alignment score formula:
```
Alignment Score = |Marketing Focus % - Revenue Contribution %|
```

Scoring:
- Aligned: <10% difference
- Misaligned: 10-20% difference
- Critical Mismatch: >20% difference

When a segment is underinvested (revenue contribution significantly exceeds marketing focus), reallocate budget from underperforming channels.

## Lead Quality Scoring Methodology

Sales rates each SQL on a 1-10 scale based on four dimensions:
- **Budget clarity:** Does the prospect have confirmed budget?
- **Decision-maker access:** Is the decision-maker engaged?
- **Timeline urgency:** How soon does the prospect need a solution?
- **Technical fit:** Does the prospect's use case match Layaa AI capabilities?

Track average lead quality score by campaign to identify which sources produce high-intent, well-qualified leads vs. tire-kickers.

## Common GTM-Revenue Misalignment Patterns

### Pattern 1: Marketing Overpromises, Sales Cannot Deliver

Detection: Sales reports >3 deals lost due to timeline or capability objections that trace back to marketing claims.

Example: Marketing says "AI Automation in 2 Weeks" but implementation takes 4-6 weeks minimum.

Correction:
- Escalate to Marketing leadership
- Adjust messaging to reflect realistic timelines
- Update all content assets with corrected claims

### Pattern 2: Sales Selling Outside ICP, Delivery Struggles

Detection: Delivery flags "scope mismatch" at project kickoff. Client size or complexity exceeds team capacity.

Example: Sales closes deal with 500+ employee enterprise when ICP is 5-200 employees.

Correction:
- Document ICP violation
- Reinforce ICP qualification criteria in sales process
- Strategic decision: honor contract with adjusted resources or renegotiate scope

Prevention: Add ICP checklist to sales enablement SOP.

### Pattern 3: Marketing Targets ICP, But Pricing Does Not Fit

Detection: MQL to SQL conversion <15% for a specific campaign. High lead volume but budget mismatches dominate lost reasons.

Example: Campaign targets micro-SMEs (5-10 employees) but minimum implementation is Rs 50k, which exceeds their budget.

Correction options:
- A) Create a lower pricing tier for the targeted segment
- B) Shift marketing to target larger SMEs that can afford current pricing

Decision requires strategic leadership and Founder approval.

## Monthly Alignment Review Checklist

Review date: 10th of each month (reviewing previous month).

1. Compile campaign performance data (MQLs, SQLs, Revenue)
2. Calculate ROI for each active campaign (spend data from performance marketing)
3. Analyze ICP targeting overlap (marketing budget allocation vs. revenue contribution)
4. Review channel ROI variance (identify underperformers)
5. Audit lead quality scores (SQL quality ratings from sales)
6. Identify GTM-Revenue misalignments (overpromises, ICP drift, pricing mismatch)
7. Generate GTM-Revenue Alignment Report
8. Share with strategic leadership and marketing for adjustments

## GTM-Revenue Alignment Report Template

Structure:

**Campaign Performance Summary**
- Total marketing spend
- Total revenue influenced
- Blended ROI
- Top performers (campaign name, ROI, revenue influenced)
- Underperformers (campaign name, ROI, recommendation: pause / optimize / reallocate)

**ICP Alignment Analysis**
- Per-ICP comparison: Marketing focus % vs. Revenue contribution %
- Alignment status per segment
- Reallocation recommendations

**Channel ROI Variance**
- Best channels by ROI
- Worst channels by ROI with recommended actions

**Lead Quality Assessment**
- Average lead quality score across campaigns
- Per-campaign quality breakdown
- Campaigns below quality threshold flagged

**Misalignments Detected**
- Misalignment type and description
- Root cause analysis
- Proposed fix
- Escalation status

**Recommendations**
- Numbered action items for the coming period
