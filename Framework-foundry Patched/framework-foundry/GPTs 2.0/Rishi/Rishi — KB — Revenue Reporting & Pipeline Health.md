## Pipeline Health Indicators

### Green (Healthy)
- All conversion rates within or above target
- Pipeline coverage above 3x
- No deals stalled >14 days
- Forecast accuracy within 15%
- Balanced stage distribution (not all deals at same stage)

### Yellow (Monitor)
- One conversion rate in yellow zone
- Pipeline coverage 2-3x
- 1-2 deals approaching stall threshold
- Forecast accuracy 15-30% variance
- Stage distribution skewed (too many MQLs, not enough Proposals)

### Red (Intervene)
- Any conversion rate in red zone
- Pipeline coverage below 2x
- 3+ deals stalled or end-to-end conversion below 3%
- Forecast accuracy above 30% variance
- Revenue target at risk for current quarter

### Intervention Protocol
1. **Identify** — Which specific metric triggered the red flag?
2. **Diagnose** — Is it a quality issue (bad leads), a process issue (slow follow-up), or a market issue (competitive pressure)?
3. **Route** — Direct to the right specialist (@Mira for lead quality, @Yuvaan for sales process, @Veer for pricing, @Kabir for systemic)
4. **Track** — Create a task for the intervention and monitor resolution
5. **Learn** — Once resolved, document the pattern and fix for future reference
