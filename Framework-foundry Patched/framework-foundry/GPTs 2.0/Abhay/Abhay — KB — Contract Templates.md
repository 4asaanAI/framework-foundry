# Abhay — Contract Templates

> This document contains Layaa AI's standard contract templates. These are structural frameworks — populate with the appropriate clause language from the Legal Clause Library when drafting actual agreements.

## Template A: Master Services Agreement (MSA) — Structure

```
MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

Date: [Date]
Between:
(1) [Client Legal Name], [CIN/Registration], [Address] ("Client")
(2) Layaa AI Private Limited, CIN U62099HR2025PTC139528,
    Plot No. A16, First Floor, Ashok Estate, Sector-63-A,
    Gurgaon, Haryana 122102 ("Provider")

RECITALS
A. The Provider is engaged in AI automation and enablement services.
B. The Client wishes to engage the Provider for certain services
   as described in Statements of Work to be executed hereunder.

1. DEFINITIONS
   1.1 "Agreement" means this MSA and all SoWs executed hereunder.
   1.2 "Deliverables" means...
   1.3 "Services" means...
   [Expand with all defined terms]

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES [Clause 1]
3. TERM AND RENEWAL
4. FEES AND PAYMENT [Clause 4]
5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY [Clause 2]
6. CONFIDENTIALITY [Clause 3]
7. DATA PROTECTION [Clause 8]
8. WARRANTIES AND DISCLAIMERS [Clause 11]
9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY [Clause 5]
10. INDEMNIFICATION [Clause 6]
11. TERMINATION [Clause 7]
12. FORCE MAJEURE [Clause 9]
13. NON-SOLICITATION [Clause 12]
14. DISPUTE RESOLUTION [Clause 10]
15. GENERAL PROVISIONS
    15.1 Entire Agreement
    15.2 Amendment (written only)
    15.3 Assignment
    15.4 Severability
    15.5 Notices
    15.6 Counterparts
    15.7 Governing Law

SCHEDULE A: Statement of Work [Template B]

Signed by:
For and on behalf of [Client]:          For and on behalf of Layaa AI Pvt Ltd:
Name:                                    Name:
```

## Template B: Statement of Work (SoW) — Structure

```
STATEMENT OF WORK #[Number]
Under the Master Services Agreement dated [Date]

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
   1.1 Project Name
   1.2 Project Objective
   1.3 Background

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
   2.1 In-Scope Services [detailed description]
   2.2 Out-of-Scope [explicit exclusions]
   2.3 Assumptions

3. DELIVERABLES
   [Table: Deliverable | Description | Acceptance Criteria | Due Date]

4. TIMELINE AND MILESTONES
   [Table: Milestone | Description | Target Date]

5. FEES AND PAYMENT SCHEDULE
   5.1 Implementation Fee: Rs.[Amount] + GST
   5.2 Payment Schedule:
       - 50% on SoW execution: Rs.[Amount]
       - 50% on delivery and acceptance: Rs.[Amount]
   5.3 Monthly Retainer (if applicable): Rs.[Amount]/month + GST
   5.4 Payment Terms: [15/30] days from invoice date

6. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES
   [What the client must provide: access, data, personnel, decisions]

7. CHANGE MANAGEMENT
   [Change Order process with pricing adjustment mechanism]

8. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
   [How deliverables will be tested and accepted]

9. PROJECT CONTACTS
   [Table: Role | Name | Email | Phone]
```

## Template C: Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) — Structure

```
NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

Mutual / One-Way (select appropriate)
Effective Date: [Date]
Between: [Party 1] and [Party 2]

1. DEFINITIONS
   1.1 "Confidential Information" means...
   1.2 Exclusions...

2. OBLIGATIONS
3. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES
4. TERM: [1-3 years]
5. RETURN/DESTRUCTION OF INFORMATION
6. NO LICENSE GRANTED
7. REMEDIES (injunctive relief)
8. GOVERNING LAW (India)
9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION (Arbitration, Gurgaon)

Duration guidance:
- Exploratory discussions: 1 year
- Active engagements: 2-3 years
- Trade secrets: perpetual
```

## Template D: Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) — Structure

```
MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

Date: [Date]
Between: [Party 1] and [Party 2]

1. PURPOSE AND BACKGROUND
2. AREAS OF COLLABORATION
3. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
4. TERMS AND CONDITIONS
5. CONFIDENTIALITY
6. NON-BINDING NATURE [critical — MoU is typically non-binding]
   "This MoU represents the parties' intention to collaborate
    and does not create binding legal obligations except for
    Clauses [Confidentiality, Governing Law]."
7. TERM AND TERMINATION
8. GOVERNING LAW
```
