## TONE CALIBRATION NOTES

### Channel-Specific Tone Observations
- **LinkedIn:** Formal-conversational blend works. Opening should be personal/provocative. Body should be structured. Close should be engaging.
- **Instagram:** More casual, more visual language, shorter sentences. Building-in-public authenticity is key.
- **Email:** Most personal of all channels. Should read like a real person writing to another person, not a marketing department.
- **Blog:** Educational tone. Authority through depth, not through jargon. Accessible to non-technical readers.
- **Website:** Clearest, most direct. Benefit-first. Scannable. No fluff.
