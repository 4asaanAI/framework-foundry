## Filing Checklists — Quick Reference

### Pre-AGM Checklist
- [ ] Financial statements audited and signed
- [ ] Board's Report drafted and approved by Board
- [ ] Auditor's Report received
- [ ] Notice of AGM prepared (21 clear days before AGM for general business)
- [ ] Agenda items finalized
- [ ] Venue/virtual meeting arrangements confirmed
- [ ] Draft resolutions prepared (adoption of accounts, auditor appointment, etc.)

### Post-AGM Checklist
- [ ] Minutes of AGM drafted and signed (within 30 days)
- [ ] ADT-1 filed (within 15 days of AGM)
- [ ] AOC-4 filed (within 30 days of AGM)
- [ ] MGT-7/7A filed (within 60 days of AGM)
- [ ] Statutory registers updated
- [ ] All resolutions filed with ROC (if required)

### Monthly Compliance Check
- [ ] GST data prepared and shared with CA (by 15th)
- [ ] TDS deposited (by 7th)
- [ ] Bank reconciliation completed (Aarav)
- [ ] Any new contracts requiring compliance review flagged to Abhay
- [ ] Compliance calendar reviewed for upcoming deadlines

## Escalation Protocol

### Compliance Escalation Matrix

| Severity | Condition | Escalation Path | Timeline |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| **Critical** | Filing deadline will be missed | Anne → Kabir → Founders IMMEDIATELY | Same day |
| **High** | Filing deadline within 7 days, preparation incomplete | Anne → Kabir → Founders | Same day |
| **Medium** | Filing deadline within 15 days, minor gaps in preparation | Anne → Kabir | Within 24 hours |
| **Low** | Filing deadline within 30 days, on track | Standard tracking | Weekly update |
