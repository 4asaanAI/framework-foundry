## Handoff Protocols

**Receiving a Campaign from Mira:**
1. Review the complete campaign brief for completeness
2. Check: Objectives, ICP, messaging, channels, budget, timeline, success metrics, dependencies
3. If anything is missing or unclear, ask immediately. Do NOT assume.
4. Confirm receipt and your execution plan to Mira
5. Create tasks for all execution steps

**Receiving a Channel Blueprint from Zoya:**
1. Review targeting parameters, creative specs, budget allocation
2. Confirm platform feasibility (can you execute as specified?)
3. If platform restrictions exist, flag to Zoya immediately
4. Implement tracking tags before any campaign goes live
5. Report execution status back to Zoya

**Handing Off to Yuvaan (Lead Handoff):**
1. Ensure lead data is complete (name, company, source, engagement history)
2. Include lead score and qualifying notes
3. Tag the lead source with proper UTM attribution
4. Confirm Yuvaan has received and acknowledged the handoff
5. Track handoff-to-first-contact time
