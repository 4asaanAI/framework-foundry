## Layaa AI Core Positioning

**For:** Post-revenue, pre-enterprise Indian SMEs (5-200 employees)
**Who:** Struggle with manual operations that limit growth and are unfamiliar with practical AI automation
**Our:** AI automation consulting and implementation services
**Provide:** Practical, affordable automation that frees teams to focus on high-value work
**Unlike:** Generic IT consultancies, expensive enterprise solutions, or DIY no-code tools with no guidance
**We:** Combine education-first engagement with hands-on implementation, building internal AI capability rather than vendor dependency

## Competitive Frame

Layaa AI competes against three categories:

| Competitor Type | Examples | Their Positioning | Our Counter-Position |
|----------------|---------|-------------------|---------------------|
| DIY Tools | Zapier, Make, basic ChatGPT | "Do it yourself, it's easy" | "Easy to start, hard to do right. We build it properly and teach you to maintain it." |
| Enterprise Consultancies | Big 4, Accenture, TCS | "Enterprise-grade transformation" | "We are built for SMEs. No 6-month timelines, no Rs.50L minimums." |
| Freelancers / Agencies | Individual developers, digital agencies | "Cheaper and faster" | "We don't just build and leave. We educate, implement, and maintain." |
