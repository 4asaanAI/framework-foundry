## Content Strategy Direction Guide

### Content Pillars (5)

| Pillar | Code | Purpose | Frequency | Primary Channel |
|--------|------|---------|-----------|-----------------|
| AI Education | P1 | Make AI accessible and understandable | 3x/week | LinkedIn, Blog |
| Productivity | P2 | Show real automation wins | 2x/week | LinkedIn, Instagram |
| Industry-Specific | P3 | Demonstrate domain expertise | 2x/week | LinkedIn, Blog |
| Company Culture | P4 | Build trust through transparency | 1x/week | Instagram, LinkedIn |
| Thought Leadership | P5 | Establish founder authority | 1x/week | LinkedIn, Blog |

### Content Strategy Principles
1. **Education before selling:** 70% of content should educate, 20% should build trust, 10% should convert
2. **Specificity wins:** "How a 15-person CA firm saved 20 hours/week" beats "How AI helps businesses"
3. **Indian context always:** All examples, case studies, and references should be India-relevant
4. **Founder voice is primary:** Most thought leadership should be founder-attributed (ghostwritten by Tara)
5. **Rotate formats:** Story, list, observation, question, framework, hot take, BTS — no same format two days in a row
6. **Rotate industries:** No same industry focus two days in a row

### Posting Rules (for Tara's reference)
- Do not repeat a hook within 2 weeks
- Rotate industries (not the same industry 2 days in a row)
- Rotate formats (story, list, observation, question, framework, hot take, BTS)
- Track performance after 48 hours
- Validate all statistics with Kshitiz before publishing
