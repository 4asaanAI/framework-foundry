- SOM (3 years): 50-100 SME clients, Rs.50L-1Cr revenue

### SME AI Adoption Barriers
1. **Awareness Gap:** Most SMEs do not know what AI automation can do for them
2. **Trust Deficit:** Previous bad experiences with IT vendors, fear of complexity
3. **Budget Constraints:** Limited discretionary budget for technology investments
4. **Skill Gap:** No internal technical team to evaluate or manage AI solutions
5. **Change Resistance:** Established processes are "good enough" — why change?

### Layaa AI's Response to Each Barrier
1. Awareness → Education-first GTM (workshops, content, thought leadership)
2. Trust → Transparent pricing, clear timelines, pilot programs
3. Budget → Tiered pricing (Starter Rs.15K/mo), ROI-driven positioning
4. Skill → We build AND teach (education + implementation)
5. Change → Start small (one process), demonstrate value, expand

- **Long-term:** All Tier 1 cities + select Tier 2

## ICP DEEP DIVES

### ICP 1: SaaS Startups (5-25 employees)
- **Stage:** Post-seed, pre-Series A
- **Pain:** Manual operations (onboarding, support, billing) slowing growth
- **Budget:** Rs.2-5L implementation, medium-high willingness to pay
- **Sales Cycle:** 30-50 days
- **Decision Maker:** Founder/CEO or Head of Ops
- **Buying Triggers:** Hiring freeze, scaling pressure, investor expectations
- **Channel:** LinkedIn, content marketing, startup community events
- **Messaging:** "Scale operations without scaling headcount"

### ICP 2: Logistics & Supply Chain SMEs (50-200 employees)
- **Stage:** Established, revenue Rs.1Cr+
- **Pain:** Manual tracking, coordination failures, error rates
- **Budget:** ROI-driven (need to see numbers before committing)
- **Sales Cycle:** 60-90 days
- **Decision Maker:** Operations Head or Managing Director
- **Buying Triggers:** Lost shipments, growing error rates, competitor modernization
- **Channel:** Industry events, referrals, LinkedIn
- **Messaging:** "Bring visibility and automation to your operations"

### ICP 3: Fintech & Payment Processors (10-100 employees)
- **Stage:** Growing, regulated environment
- **Pain:** Regulatory reporting, fraud detection, compliance overhead
- **Budget:** Medium-High
- **Sales Cycle:** 45-75 days
- **Decision Maker:** CTO or Compliance Head
- **Buying Triggers:** Regulatory changes, audit findings, scaling compliance team
- **Channel:** LinkedIn thought leadership, Google Ads (intent-based)
- **Messaging:** "Automate compliance without compromising accuracy"

### ICP 4: Professional Services — HR/Legal/Consulting (10-50 employees)
- **Stage:** Established, service-based revenue
- **Pain:** Admin overhead eating billable hours, client communication gaps
- **Budget:** Variable, often conservative
- **Sales Cycle:** 50-80 days
- **Decision Maker:** Managing Partner or Operations Manager
- **Buying Triggers:** Growing team without growing support staff, client complaints
- **Channel:** LinkedIn, email, referrals
- **Messaging:** "Give your team back 10 hours a week"
