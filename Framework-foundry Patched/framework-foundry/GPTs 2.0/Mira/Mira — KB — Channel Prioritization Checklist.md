## Channel Evaluation Criteria

Score each channel 1-5 on these dimensions:

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Reach | 20% | Access to target ICP segment |
| Cost Efficiency | 20% | Expected CAC relative to LTV |
| Speed to Lead | 15% | Time from first impression to first lead |
| Lead Quality | 20% | Fit of leads generated (ICP match score) |
| Scalability | 15% | Ability to grow without proportional cost increase |
| Control | 10% | Our influence over channel performance |

## Default Budget Allocation Framework

| Channel | Allocation | Purpose | Key Metrics |
|---------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| LinkedIn (Organic + Paid) | 30-35% | Primary awareness and thought leadership | Impressions, engagement rate, InMail response rate, MQLs |
| Google (Search + Display) | 20-25% | Intent-based capture | CTR, CPA, conversion rate, keyword rankings |
| Content / SEO | 15-20% | Long-term organic growth | Traffic, keyword rankings, time-on-page, lead magnet conversions |
| Email | 5-10% | Nurture and retention | Open rate, CTR, conversion rate, unsubscribe rate |
| Retargeting | 10-15% | Re-engagement of warm audiences | Return visit rate, conversion rate, cost per conversion |
| Experimental | 10% | Testing new channels (WhatsApp, events, partnerships) | Varied — defined per experiment |

## Channel Prioritization by GTM Phase

**Phase 1 (Current — Education Beachhead):**
- Tier 1: WhatsApp outreach, In-person meetings, Referrals
- Tier 2: LinkedIn organic (founder content)
- Tier 3: Website, Basic SEO

**Phase 2 (Adjacent Verticals):**
- Tier 1: LinkedIn (organic + paid), Content/SEO
- Tier 2: Google Search Ads, Email nurture
- Tier 3: Partnerships, Events

**Phase 3 (Geographic Expansion):**
- Tier 1: LinkedIn Paid, Google Ads, Content/SEO
- Tier 2: Retargeting, Email, Partnerships
- Tier 3: PR, Events, WhatsApp Business
