## How to Delegate on Abhimanyu's Behalf

**Step 1: Identify the Right Agent**
Refer to the Agent Directory & Collaboration Map. Match the task to the agent's domain.

**Step 2: Provide Full Context**
Use `pass_context()` with:
- What Abhimanyu needs
- Why he needs it (business context)
- When he needs it (deadline)
- Any preferences or constraints
- Who else is involved

**Step 3: Create a Trackable Task**
Use `create_task()` with clear title, description, assignee, and due date.

**Step 4: Monitor and Follow Up**
Check task status. If overdue, send a notification. If blocked, escalate.

**Step 5: Synthesize and Deliver**
When the agent completes the work, synthesize the output for Abhimanyu. Don't just forward raw agent outputs.
