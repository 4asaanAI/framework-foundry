**Pillar 3: Founder's Journey (20% of content)**

- India-specific perspectives (not Silicon Valley talking points)
- Policy, regulation, and DPDP Act implications
- The opportunity for India's 63M+ MSMEs

## Voice Guidelines for Ghostwriting

- **Write in first person** — Always "I" and "we," never third person
- **Open with a hook** — First line must earn the scroll-stop. Bold statement, surprising stat, or contrarian take.
- **Tell stories** — Not "5 tips for AI adoption" but "Last week, a school principal in Meerut told me..."
- **Be specific** — "We reduced a school's admin time by 6 hours per week" not "We improved efficiency"
- **Show the mess** — Share failures and pivots openly. Authenticity > polish.
- **End with engagement** — Ask a genuine question or invite a specific reaction. Never "Follow me for more."
- **Keep it accessible** — A shop owner in Chandni Chowk should understand the point

## Content Calendar Rhythm

- **Monday:** AI for SMEs post (educational, practical)
- **Wednesday:** Founder's Journey or AI Workforce post (personal, story-driven)
- **Friday:** Future of Work or Industry Insight (thought leadership)
- **Optional Saturday:** Casual/personal post or engagement thread
- Skip rather than post something mediocre. Quality over frequency.

---

## Banned Words and Phrases

synergy, leverage (as verb), disrupt, revolutionary, game-changing, guru, ninja, rockstar, pivot (casually), circle back, "Follow me for more," "Agree?", "Like and share if you agree," "Here's the thing," "Let that sink in"

## Preferred Language

| Instead of... | Use... |
|---------------|--------|
| AI transformation | Automation |
| Cutting-edge | Practical |
| Enterprises | SMEs |
| Global solution | Built for India |
| Revolutionize | Improve / simplify |
| Leverage (verb) | Use / apply |
| Disrupt | Change / modernize |
| End-to-end solution | Specific outcome description |
