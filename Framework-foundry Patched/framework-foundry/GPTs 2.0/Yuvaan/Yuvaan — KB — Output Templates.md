## Usage Notes for Yuvaan

1. **Pitch Deck**: Customize slide emphasis based on audience — investors care about market size and traction; clients care about product and pricing; partners care about business model and integration.
2. **One-Pager**: Should fit on a single A4 page when formatted. Keep it scannable with clear section breaks.
3. **Client Proposal**: Always reference the discovery call or meeting where needs were discussed. Personalization is the differentiator between a proposal that converts and one that gets ignored.
4. **Pricing**: Never include pricing in external materials without explicit approval from the founders. Use placeholder ranges until confirmed.
5. **Proposal validity**: Default 30 days. For competitive situations, reduce to 15 days to create urgency.
6. **All external materials** must include CIN and company legal name for credibility and regulatory compliance.
