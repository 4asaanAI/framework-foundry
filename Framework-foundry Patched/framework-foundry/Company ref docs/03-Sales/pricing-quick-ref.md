## 3. Hybrid Pricing Formula

For complex engagements, use the hybrid pricing model:

```
Total Price = Fixed Component + Variable Component + Value-Based Component
```

### Components

| Component | Description | When to Use |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Fixed** | Base implementation fee for defined scope | Always -- core deliverables |
| **Variable** | Per-unit or per-hour charges for scope that may flex | When scope has known variability (e.g., number of workflows, data volume) |
| **Value-Based** | Success fee tied to measurable outcome | When client wants skin-in-the-game pricing; deal > â‚ą3L |

## 4. Discount Policy

### Discount Authority Matrix

| Discount Level | Authority | Conditions |
|----------------|-----------|------------|
| 0-5% | AE discretion | Any deal; no approval needed |
| 6-10% | AE discretion | Requires justification in CRM notes (annual prepay, multi-module, referral) |
| 11-15% | Sales Lead approval | Annual prepayment AND (case study rights OR referral commitment) |
| 16-20% | Founder approval | Strategic account; documented LTV > â‚ą10L; written justification |
| >20% | **Founder explicit approval** | **Exceptional circumstances only. Must be justified in writing with full deal economics.** |

### Discount Rules

1. **Never discount the retainer.** Retainers are recurring revenue -- protect them. Offer implementation discounts instead.
2. **Trade, don't give.** Every discount must come with a reciprocal commitment:
   - 5% discount â†’ Annual retainer prepayment
   - 10% discount â†’ Case study + referral + annual prepay
   - 15% discount â†’ All of the above + 2-year commitment
3. **Volume discounts** for multi-module deals: 5% for 2 modules, 10% for 3+ modules (applied to implementation only).
4. **Early payment discount:** 2% for payment within 7 days of invoice.
5. **Startup discount:** 10% for companies < 2 years old with funding proof. Cannot combine with other discounts.

| Service Vertical | Target Margin | Minimum Acceptable | Pricing Lever |
|-----------------|---------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Education & Training | 50-60% | 50% | Content reuse, template-based delivery |
| Consulting & Advisory | 40-50% | 40% | Senior time allocation, deliverable templating |
| Automation Development | 35-45% | 35% | Component reuse, Layaa OS platform |
| Maintenance & Support | 60-70% | 60% | Automation of monitoring, batch servicing |
| Pre-Built Automations | 70%+ | 70% | Zero marginal cost per deployment |

### Margin Calculation

```
Gross Margin = (Revenue - Direct Costs) / Revenue x 100

Direct Costs include:
- Team hours x internal hourly rate
- Third-party tools/API costs passed to project
- Infrastructure costs for client environment
- Travel/on-site costs (if applicable)

Direct Costs DO NOT include:
- Sales commission
- Marketing costs
- General overhead
- Founder time (unless explicitly allocated to project)
```
